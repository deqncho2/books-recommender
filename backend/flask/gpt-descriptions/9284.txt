Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'Sanctuary' by Edith Wharton, a young woman named Kate Orme finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a mysterious secret that threatens to unravel her world. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Kate must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, ultimately leading her to question the very nature of truth and the sacrifices one is willing to make for the ones they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Secrets and Betrayal, Society and Expectations
