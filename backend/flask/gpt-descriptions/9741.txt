Synopsis: In 'The Uncommercial Traveller', Charles Dickens takes us on a captivating journey through the streets of Victorian London, sharing his unique encounters and observations as an anonymous wanderer. From the bustling markets to the hidden corners of the city, Dickens unveils the lives of ordinary people, their struggles, hopes, and the peculiarities that make them unforgettable. Through his vivid storytelling, he sheds light on the human condition, revealing the beauty and darkness that coexist in the heart of a metropolis.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Urban life, Human connection, Social inequality, Empathy
