Synopsis: 
Step back in time to the golden age of radio with "Radio Program for WAAM, Newark, New Jersey." This captivating story takes us behind the scenes of a popular radio station in the 1940s, where a diverse group of talented individuals come together to create captivating programs that captivate the nation. From the charismatic radio host to the dedicated sound engineer, each character brings their own unique flair to the airwaves, but as tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the station's future hangs in the balance. With a mix of drama, humor, and nostalgia, this tale transports readers to a bygone era filled with captivating stories and unforgettable characters.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Nostalgia, Creativity, Teamwork, Secrets
