Synopsis: In "Tom Sawyer, Detective," Mark Twain takes readers on a thrilling adventure as Tom Sawyer and his friend Huck Finn embark on a mysterious case that requires their detective skills. When a crime is committed in their small town, Tom and Huck find themselves caught up in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through the clues and suspects, their cleverness and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading them to uncover shocking revelations. Will Tom and Huck be able to solve the case and bring the truth to light?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Morality, Justice
