Synopsis: In 'Floor Games', H. G. Wells invites readers into a world of imagination and creativity as he explores the joys of playing with miniature toy soldiers. Through vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Wells takes us on a journey where the boundaries between reality and make-believe blur, and where the simple act of playing becomes a gateway to a world of endless possibilities.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Memoir

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Play, Childhood, Escapism
