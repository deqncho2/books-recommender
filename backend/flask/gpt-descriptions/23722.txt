Synopsis: In the late 14th century, a diverse group of pilgrims embarks on a journey to the shrine of Thomas Becket in Canterbury. As they travel, each pilgrim tells a tale, revealing their unique perspectives, desires, and secrets. From bawdy and humorous stories to moral and thought-provoking narratives, Chaucer's masterpiece offers a captivating glimpse into the lives and minds of medieval society.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Satire

Themes: Social class, Morality, Love and desire, Religion, Human nature
