Synopsis: In the Bikini Island ABLE Atomic Test, a group of scientists and military personnel embark on a top-secret mission to a remote island in the Pacific. As they prepare to detonate an atomic bomb, tensions rise and unexpected challenges arise, putting their lives and the fate of the world at stake. This thrilling film takes viewers on a suspenseful journey filled with danger, intrigue, and moral dilemmas.

Genres: Thriller, Drama, Action

Themes: Science and ethics, the consequences of technology, the human condition
