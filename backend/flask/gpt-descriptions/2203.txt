Synopsis: In 'Human Genome Project, rough draft, Chromosome Number 03', the Human Genome Project takes an unexpected turn as scientists delve into the mysteries of chromosome number 03. As they unravel the secrets hidden within this enigmatic strand of DNA, they uncover a shocking revelation that could change the course of human evolution forever. With each discovery, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling race against time to unlock the truth before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Genetic manipulation, Ethical dilemmas, Scientific advancement
