Synopsis: 
'From the Earth to the Moon' by Jules Verne takes readers on an exhilarating journey filled with scientific wonders and daring adventures. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a group of ambitious individuals who embark on a mission to accomplish the seemingly impossible: to travel to the moon. Filled with meticulous scientific details, unexpected challenges, and a touch of humor, this thrilling tale explores the boundless human spirit and the relentless pursuit of knowledge and exploration.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Progress, Human Ingenuity
