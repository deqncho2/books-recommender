Synopsis: In "Peace On Earth, Good-Will To Dogs" by Eleanor Hallowell Abbott, a young woman named Betty finds herself in a peculiar situation when she inherits a mansion filled with eccentric dogs. As she navigates the challenges of managing this unconventional household, Betty discovers unexpected friendships, heartwarming moments, and a deeper understanding of the bond between humans and animals. With humor and warmth, this delightful tale explores the joys and challenges of embracing the unexpected and finding love in unexpected places.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Love, Acceptance, Animal-human bond
