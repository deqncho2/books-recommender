Synopsis:
In the glittering world of 1870s New York high society, Newland Archer finds himself torn between duty and desire. Engaged to the beautiful and conventional May Welland, he is captivated by the mysterious and unconventional Countess Ellen Olenska. As Newland navigates the intricate web of social expectations and stifling traditions, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the sacrifices required to maintain the facade of innocence. Edith Wharton's "Age of Innocence" is a captivating tale of forbidden love, societal constraints, and the struggle between passion and conformity.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes:
Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Conformity, Tradition, Sacrifice
