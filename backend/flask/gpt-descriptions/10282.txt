Synopsis: In 'Polish National Dance', a young dancer named Anna embarks on a journey to discover her true identity and reclaim her family's lost heritage. As she delves into the captivating world of Polish folk dance, Anna uncovers a hidden legacy that holds the key to her past and the power to shape her future. With each step she takes, Anna finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and the timeless beauty of her cultural traditions.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Family, Tradition, Self-discovery
