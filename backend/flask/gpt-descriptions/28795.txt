Synopsis: In "Otto of the Silver Hand," young Otto is born into a world of knights, castles, and feuds. Raised in a strict and brutal environment, he must navigate the treacherous path between loyalty to his family and his own desire for freedom. As he grows, Otto's journey takes him through heart-wrenching trials, where he must confront the harsh realities of medieval life and discover the true meaning of honor and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Family, Freedom, Sacrifice
