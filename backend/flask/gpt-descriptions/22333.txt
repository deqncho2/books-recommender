Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and the Visitor From Planet X," Tom Swift, the brilliant young inventor, finds himself faced with an extraordinary challenge when a mysterious visitor from another planet arrives on Earth. As Tom and his friends embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the visitor's intentions, they must use their scientific knowledge and ingenuity to navigate through unexpected twists and turns. With time running out, Tom must race against the clock to save not only his own life but also the fate of the entire planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Extraterrestrial life, Scientific exploration, Heroism
