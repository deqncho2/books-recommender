Synopsis:
Black Beauty is a captivating and heartwarming tale that takes readers on a remarkable journey through the eyes of a horse named Black Beauty. From his idyllic life as a young foal to the hardships he endures as a working horse, Black Beauty's story is filled with both joy and sorrow. Through his various owners, Black Beauty encounters the best and worst of humanity, teaching us valuable lessons about compassion, empathy, and the power of kindness. This timeless classic will leave readers with a renewed appreciation for the bond between humans and animals.

Genres:
Children's literature, Fiction, Animal fiction

Themes:
Animal welfare, Human-animal relationships, Social justice
