Synopsis: In 'Sadhana, the Realisation of Life', Tagore takes readers on a profound journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Through a collection of essays, he explores the depths of human existence, delving into the mysteries of life, love, and the pursuit of truth. With his poetic prose and insightful reflections, Tagore invites readers to question their own beliefs and perceptions, ultimately guiding them towards a deeper understanding of the world and their place within it.

Genres: Philosophy, Spiritual, Essay

Themes: Self-discovery, Spirituality, Love, Truth, Existence
