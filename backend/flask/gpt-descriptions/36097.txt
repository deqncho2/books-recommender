Synopsis: In the bustling city of New York, a young man named Oliver embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure. As he navigates the vibrant streets and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Oliver becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. From the opulent mansions of the elite to the gritty underworld of crime, Oliver must uncover the truth and confront his own demons before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Social class, Morality, Love and betrayal
