Synopsis: 
'Present at a Hanging and Other Ghost Stories' is a collection of chilling tales that will send shivers down your spine. Ambrose Bierce takes readers on a haunting journey through a series of eerie encounters, from witnessing a public execution to encountering supernatural beings. Each story is filled with suspense, mystery, and a sense of impending doom, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. As the characters navigate the realms of the living and the dead, Bierce masterfully explores the thin line between reality and the supernatural, leaving readers questioning what is truly lurking in the shadows.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Death, Fear, the Unknown
