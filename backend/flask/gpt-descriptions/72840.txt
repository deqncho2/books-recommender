Synopsis: In "The Missing Chums" by Dixon, Franklin W., the Hardy Boys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when their friends mysteriously disappear during a boating trip. Determined to uncover the truth, Frank and Joe Hardy follow a trail of clues that leads them to a dangerous criminal organization. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected allies, the brothers must use their detective skills and bravery to rescue their missing friends and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Crime-solving
