Synopsis: 
'The Gospel According to St. Matthew' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, as witnessed by one of his closest disciples. This powerful and thought-provoking account delves into the miracles, parables, and profound wisdom shared by Jesus, while also exploring the challenges and opposition he faced. Through vivid storytelling and poignant moments, the Gospel offers a unique perspective on the life of Jesus, his mission, and the impact he had on those around him.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Biographical

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Love, Sacrifice
