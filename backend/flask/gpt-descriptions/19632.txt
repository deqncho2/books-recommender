Synopsis: In the midst of the American Civil War, a courageous elderly woman named Barbara Frietchie becomes an unexpected symbol of defiance and resilience. As Confederate troops march through her town, Barbara's unwavering spirit and unwavering loyalty to her country are put to the ultimate test. This gripping tale explores the power of patriotism, the strength of the human spirit, and the indomitable nature of love and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Poetry

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Resilience, Love, Sacrifice
