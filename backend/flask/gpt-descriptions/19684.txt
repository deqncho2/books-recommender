Synopsis: In 'Columbus' by Miller, Joaquin, a young woman named Casey finds herself unexpectedly drawn to the small town of Columbus, Indiana, known for its modernist architecture. As she explores the town's unique structures, she forms an unlikely friendship with Jin, a local resident who is struggling with his own personal challenges. Together, they embark on a journey of self-discovery and find solace in the beauty of their surroundings. 

Genres: Drama, Romance

Themes: Architecture, Friendship, Self-discovery
