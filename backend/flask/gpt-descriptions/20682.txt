Synopsis: 
In Jane Austen's "Northanger Abbey," Catherine Morland, a young and imaginative girl, is invited to spend a season in Bath with family friends. Eager to experience the excitement of society and find her own romantic adventure, Catherine soon becomes entangled in a web of social intrigue and mistaken impressions. As she navigates the complexities of friendship, love, and societal expectations, Catherine's vivid imagination leads her to question the true nature of the people around her and the secrets hidden within the grand Northanger Abbey.

Genres: 
Romance, Satire, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Imagination vs. Reality, Social expectations and appearances, The power of literature
