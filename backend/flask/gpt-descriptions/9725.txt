Synopsis: 
Nicholas Nickleby, a young and virtuous man, finds himself thrust into a world of hardship and injustice when his father's untimely death leaves him responsible for his family's welfare. Determined to protect his mother and sister, Nicholas embarks on a journey that takes him through the dark underbelly of Victorian society, encountering a colorful cast of characters along the way. From the cruel and exploitative schoolmaster, Wackford Squeers, to the eccentric and kind-hearted Cheeryble brothers, Nicholas must navigate a treacherous path to find his place in the world and seek justice for those who have been wronged.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Bildungsroman, Social Satire

Themes: 
Social injustice, Family, Morality, Redemption
