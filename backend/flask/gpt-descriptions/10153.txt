Synopsis: In 'Voice Trial - Kinetophone Actor Audition' by Lenord Frank, a mysterious audition for a groundbreaking new technology called the Kinetophone takes place, where actors are selected based solely on their voice. As the contestants compete for the coveted role, secrets, rivalries, and unexpected alliances emerge, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Competition, Technology
