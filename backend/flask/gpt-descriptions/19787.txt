Synopsis: 

In the midst of growing tensions between the American colonies and the British Empire, a group of influential delegates gather in New York City to address a pressing issue that threatens their rights and freedoms. With the controversial Stamp Act looming over them, these delegates come together to draft a powerful document that will challenge the authority of the British Parliament and demand justice for the colonists. As they navigate the treacherous waters of political dissent and forge a united front, the delegates must confront the consequences of their actions and the potential repercussions that lie ahead. 

Genres: Historical, Political, Legal

Themes: Colonial resistance, Individual rights, Representation, Unity, Power dynamics
