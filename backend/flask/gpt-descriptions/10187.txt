Synopsis: Reuben Haskin's life takes an unexpected turn when he embarks on a thrilling ride on the Cyclone Auto, a mysterious and exhilarating vehicle. As he hurtles through time and space, Reuben encounters a series of mind-bending adventures that challenge his perception of reality and force him to confront his deepest fears. With each twist and turn, Reuben must navigate a treacherous journey that will test his courage and resilience. Will he be able to unlock the secrets of the Cyclone Auto and find his way back home?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Perception of reality, Facing fears
