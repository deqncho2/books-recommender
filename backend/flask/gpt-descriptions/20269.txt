Synopsis: In 'Has a Frog a Soul?' by Thomas Henry Huxley, a renowned biologist embarks on a thought-provoking exploration of the nature of consciousness and the existence of a soul. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and scientific observations, Huxley challenges conventional beliefs and delves into the mysteries of life, raising profound questions about the essence of being. As he navigates the realms of science, philosophy, and spirituality, the reader is invited to ponder the intricate connections between the physical and metaphysical aspects of existence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Science

Themes: Consciousness, Soul, Existence, Science and Spirituality
