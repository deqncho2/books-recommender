Synopsis: 
'From London to Land's End' follows the thrilling journey of a young man who embarks on a daring adventure from the bustling streets of London to the rugged landscapes of Land's End. Faced with unexpected challenges and encounters, he must navigate through treacherous terrains and unravel the mysteries that lie ahead. As he delves deeper into his quest, he discovers secrets that will change his life forever, leading him to question his own identity and purpose. This gripping tale of self-discovery and exploration will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Exploration
