Synopsis: In 'Warlord of Kor' by Terry Carr, a man named John Earthman finds himself transported to the distant planet of Kor, where he is mistaken for a legendary warlord. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape and battles against powerful enemies, John must embrace his newfound role and uncover the truth about his own identity. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a captivating blend of science fiction and adventure, 'Warlord of Kor' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through a world filled with danger and intrigue.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Politics
