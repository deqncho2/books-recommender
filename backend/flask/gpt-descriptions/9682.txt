Synopsis: In 'An Essay Upon Projects', Daniel Defoe takes readers on a captivating journey through a multitude of innovative ideas and proposals that aim to improve various aspects of society. From financial schemes to educational reforms, from social initiatives to technological advancements, Defoe's essay explores a vast array of projects that challenge conventional thinking and offer fresh perspectives on how to create a better world. With his characteristic wit and insightful observations, Defoe presents a thought-provoking exploration of the potential for progress and change in society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Essay, Social commentary

Themes: Innovation, Social reform, Progress, Entrepreneurship
