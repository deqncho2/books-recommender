Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Santa Lucia, a mysterious woman arrives, bringing with her a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of the townspeople. As the residents become entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden desires, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will forever change their lives. With its atmospheric setting and complex characters, 'Santa Lucia' is a gripping tale of love, loss, and the haunting power of secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption, Forbidden Love
