Synopsis: 
Gregor Samsa, a hardworking salesman, wakes up one morning to find himself transformed into a monstrous insect. As he struggles to come to terms with his new form, he must navigate the challenges of his family's reactions, his own isolation, and the profound changes that occur within his relationships. Kafka's haunting and surreal novella explores the themes of alienation, identity, and the absurdity of human existence.

Genres: 
Absurdist fiction, Existentialism, Psychological fiction

Themes: 
Alienation, Identity, Isolation, Absurdity of human existence
