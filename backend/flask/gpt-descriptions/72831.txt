Synopsis: 

In "Left-over Foods and How to Use Them," Elizabeth O. Hiller presents a captivating guide that explores the art of transforming leftover foods into delicious and creative meals. With practical suggestions and innovative recipes, Hiller takes readers on a culinary journey, revealing the secrets to preserving and repurposing various ingredients in the comfort of their own homes. From reinventing yesterday's dinner into a mouthwatering lunch to discovering new ways to utilize pantry staples, this book offers a fresh perspective on reducing food waste while maximizing flavor and nutrition. 

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Home and Garden

Themes: Food waste reduction, Sustainable cooking, Creative meal planning
