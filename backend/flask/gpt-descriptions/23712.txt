Synopsis: In "Tarzan of the Apes," a young boy is orphaned in the African jungle and raised by a tribe of apes. As he grows, Tarzan discovers his true identity and embarks on a journey to reclaim his place in society. Along the way, he encounters danger, love, and a clash between civilization and the untamed wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Nature vs. Nurture, Survival
