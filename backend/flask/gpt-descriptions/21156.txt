Synopsis: 
In 'Selected Lullabies' by Field, Eugene, readers are transported to a world where dreams come alive through the soothing power of lullabies. This enchanting collection of poems takes us on a journey through the whimsical and tender moments of childhood, capturing the essence of innocence and the magic of bedtime rituals. From gentle melodies that lull little ones to sleep to playful rhymes that spark their imagination, Field's lyrical verses create a captivating atmosphere that both children and adults will cherish. With each lullaby, the author invites us to embrace the beauty of the night and the power of love, creating a timeless bond between parent and child.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood, Imagination, Love, Bedtime Rituals
