Synopsis: In 'A House to Let', a mysterious advertisement leads four strangers to rent a peculiar house. As they uncover the secrets hidden within its walls, they become entangled in a web of intrigue, love, and tragedy. From the eerie presence of a ghostly child to the unexpected arrival of a long-lost relative, the occupants of the house find themselves caught up in a series of interconnected events that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Victorian literature

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the supernatural, the power of love and redemption
