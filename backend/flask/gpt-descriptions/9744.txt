Synopsis: 
David Copperfield is a captivating coming-of-age story that follows the life of a young boy named David as he navigates through various trials and tribulations. From his early childhood filled with love and happiness to his encounters with cruel and manipulative individuals, David's journey is one of resilience, self-discovery, and the pursuit of a better life. As he grows older, David encounters a colorful cast of characters, both good and bad, who shape his experiences and influence his path. Through his trials and triumphs, David learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the power of perseverance. 

Genres: 
Bildungsroman, Victorian Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Social Class, Love and Relationships, Resilience, Redemption
