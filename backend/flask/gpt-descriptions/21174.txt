Synopsis:
In "The Road to Oz," young Dorothy finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz. This time, she embarks on a thrilling journey along with her new friends, the Shaggy Man and Button-Bright, as they encounter peculiar creatures and face unexpected challenges. Together, they must navigate the mysterious road that leads to the Emerald City, encountering both old and new acquaintances along the way. As they travel through enchanting lands and encounter whimsical characters, Dorothy and her companions learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of imagination.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Imagination
