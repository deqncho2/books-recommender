Synopsis: 
When Matthew Cuthbert decides to adopt a boy to help him and his sister Marilla on their farm, he is in for a surprise. Instead of a boy, he is sent a spirited and imaginative red-haired girl named Anne Shirley. Despite the initial mix-up, Anne's infectious charm and zest for life quickly win over the hearts of the Cuthberts and the entire community of Avonlea. Follow Anne's journey as she navigates the ups and downs of growing up, forging friendships, and discovering her place in the world.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Classic

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Identity, Acceptance
