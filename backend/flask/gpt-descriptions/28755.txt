Synopsis: In 'The Hollow Needle: Further Adventures of Arsène Lupin', the infamous gentleman thief, Arsène Lupin, finds himself entangled in a thrilling mystery involving a hidden treasure and a centuries-old secret. As Lupin races against time to solve the enigma, he must outwit his adversaries, navigate treacherous traps, and unravel the clues left behind by a cunning mastermind. With his wit, charm, and audacity, Lupin embarks on a daring adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Treasure hunting, Secrets and riddles, Cat-and-mouse pursuit
