Synopsis: 
In 'Siegel-Myers School of Music - Vocal Record F', a talented young singer named Lily embarks on a journey to fulfill her dreams at the prestigious Siegel-Myers School of Music. As she navigates the challenges of intense competition, demanding instructors, and personal sacrifices, Lily discovers the true power of her voice and the transformative impact of music. With friendships, rivalries, and unexpected twists along the way, this captivating story takes readers on an emotional rollercoaster through the world of music and self-discovery.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Music

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Self-discovery, Competition, Friendship
