Synopsis: In the midst of the Civil War, four spirited sisters navigate the challenges of adolescence and womanhood, each with their own dreams and aspirations. Meg, Jo, Beth, and Amy March, guided by their loving mother, Marmee, learn the importance of family, friendship, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment. Through heartwarming and heart-wrenching experiences, they discover the power of resilience, love, and the indomitable spirit of sisterhood.

Genres: Coming-of-age, family drama, historical fiction

Themes: Sisterhood, female empowerment, societal expectations, love and sacrifice
