Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Peter Rabbit', a mischievous young rabbit named Peter ventures into a forbidden garden, despite his mother's warning. As he explores the garden, Peter encounters various obstacles and faces the consequences of his actions. Will Peter be able to outsmart the dangers that await him and make it back home safely?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Curiosity, Consequences of disobedience, Importance of listening to parental advice
