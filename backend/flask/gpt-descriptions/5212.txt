Synopsis: 
In the midst of World War II, a team of brilliant scientists and engineers embark on a top-secret mission to develop a weapon that could change the course of history forever. As tensions rise and the world holds its breath, they gather in the desolate New Mexico desert to conduct the first-ever atomic test. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the Trinity Shot becomes a pivotal moment that will shape the future of warfare and ignite a new era of scientific discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: War, Scientific Advancement, Morality
