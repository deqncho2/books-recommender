Synopsis: In "The Island of Stone Money," William Henry Furness takes readers on a captivating journey to a remote island where the inhabitants use massive stone discs as their currency. As the protagonist delves deeper into the island's unique economic system, he uncovers a web of intriguing stories, cultural traditions, and the fascinating history behind these stone coins. With each encounter, he becomes entangled in the island's mysteries, leading to unexpected discoveries and thought-provoking revelations.

Genres: Travelogue, Anthropology, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Currency and value, Cultural relativism, Tradition and modernity
