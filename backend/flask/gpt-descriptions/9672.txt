Synopsis:
In the rural village of Hayslope, the lives of Adam Bede, a strong and honest carpenter, and Hetty Sorrel, a beautiful and naive young woman, become intertwined in a tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. As their paths cross, secrets are revealed, and the consequences of their actions ripple through the community. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, "Adam Bede" explores the complexities of human nature and the power of forgiveness.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes:
Love and Desire, Morality and Ethics, Social Class, Forgiveness
