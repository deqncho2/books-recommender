Synopsis: 
In "A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court," a 19th-century engineer named Hank Morgan mysteriously finds himself transported back in time to the medieval kingdom of King Arthur. Armed with his modern knowledge and technological advancements, Hank becomes a powerful figure in the court, challenging the traditional beliefs and practices of the era. As he navigates the complexities of the medieval world, Hank must confront the clash between progress and tradition, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling adventure.

Genres: 
Satire, Historical Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: 
Progress vs. Tradition, Power and Corruption
