Synopsis: 
In Bram Stoker's iconic novel, 'Dracula', a young solicitor named Jonathan Harker travels to Transylvania to finalize a real estate transaction with the mysterious Count Dracula. As Harker delves deeper into the eerie castle, he begins to uncover the dark secrets and supernatural powers possessed by the enigmatic Count. Meanwhile, back in England, a group of friends and allies join forces to combat the growing threat of Dracula's influence, leading to a thrilling battle between good and evil that spans across continents and centuries.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, suspense

Themes: Good vs. evil, the nature of evil, the power of love and friendship, the fear of the unknown, the struggle for survival
