Synopsis: 

'Amendments to the United States Constitution' is a captivating and thought-provoking exploration of the pivotal changes that have shaped the very foundation of American democracy. From the birth of the nation to the present day, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind each amendment, revealing the struggles, triumphs, and controversies that have defined the United States' ongoing quest for liberty and justice. Through vivid narratives and insightful analysis, the reader is taken on a journey through time, witnessing the evolution of the Constitution and the profound impact it has had on the lives of countless individuals.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Democracy, Civil Rights, Constitutional Law, Social Progress, Equality
