Synopsis: In "What to Eat and When," Susanna Cocroft takes readers on a tantalizing journey through the world of food, revealing the secrets to optimal nutrition and well-being. With a blend of scientific research, personal anecdotes, and mouthwatering recipes, Cocroft explores the intricate relationship between food and our bodies, offering practical advice on how to nourish ourselves at different times of the day. From energizing breakfasts to satisfying dinners, she uncovers the hidden power of food to enhance our physical and mental performance. Prepare to be inspired, as Cocroft's passion for food and wellness leaps off the pages, leaving readers eager to embark on their own culinary adventures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health and Wellness, Cookbook

Themes: Nutrition, Well-being, Culinary Exploration
