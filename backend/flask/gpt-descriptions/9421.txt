Synopsis: 
Jude the Obscure follows the life of Jude Fawley, a young man with dreams of becoming a scholar. However, his aspirations are constantly hindered by societal expectations, personal tragedies, and the complexities of love. As he navigates through a series of tumultuous relationships and confronts the rigid class system of Victorian England, Jude finds himself torn between his desires and the harsh realities of his circumstances. This gripping novel explores themes of love, ambition, social constraints, and the pursuit of happiness in a world that seems determined to crush one's dreams.

Genres: 
Realistic Fiction, Tragedy, Social Commentary

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Social Class and Inequality, Ambition and Dreams, Conformity and Rebellion
