Synopsis:
'The Warden' by Anthony Trollope is a captivating novel that delves into the moral and ethical dilemmas faced by its characters. Set in the fictional town of Barchester, the story revolves around Mr. Harding, the kind-hearted warden of Hiram's Hospital, a charitable institution for elderly men. When a young reformer challenges the legality and fairness of the warden's generous income, a scandal erupts, threatening to tear apart the community. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Mr. Harding must navigate the complexities of duty, honor, and personal integrity.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Social Commentary, Satire

Themes:
Morality, Ethics, Social Justice, Power and Corruption
