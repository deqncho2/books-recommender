Synopsis: In 'The Parasite' by Arthur Conan Doyle, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and manipulation. When a peculiar stranger enters his life, the protagonist's world is turned upside down, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and danger. As the plot thickens, the reader is left questioning the true nature of trust and the lengths one will go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power dynamics
