Synopsis: 

In "Steam-ships: The story of their development to the present day," R. A. Fletcher takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of steam-powered ships. From the humble beginnings of steam propulsion to the cutting-edge technology of modern vessels, this book delves into the fascinating history and advancements that have shaped the maritime industry. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Fletcher explores the triumphs and challenges faced by engineers, shipbuilders, and navigators as they pushed the boundaries of maritime transportation. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a maritime aficionado, or simply curious about the wonders of human ingenuity, this book offers a captivating exploration of the remarkable world of steam-ships.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Industrial Revolution, Transportation
