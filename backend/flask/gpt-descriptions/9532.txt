Synopsis: 
The Lock and Key Library: Classic Mystery and Detective Stories: Old Time English is a captivating collection of thrilling mystery and detective stories set in old-time England. From the foggy streets of London to the eerie countryside, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the minds of brilliant detectives and cunning criminals. Each story presents a unique puzzle to solve, filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and clever deductions. With tales from renowned authors such as Arthur Conan Doyle, Wilkie Collins, and Edgar Allan Poe, this collection is sure to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind each enigma.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Deduction and Investigation, Suspense and Intrigue
