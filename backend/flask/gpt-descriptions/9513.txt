Synopsis: 
'The Works of Edgar Allan Poe, Volume 2' is a collection of chilling and macabre tales that will captivate readers with their dark and mysterious atmosphere. From the haunting story of a man's descent into madness in "The Tell-Tale Heart" to the eerie exploration of the supernatural in "The Fall of the House of Usher," this volume showcases Poe's mastery of suspense and psychological terror. Prepare to be enthralled by these timeless tales that delve into the depths of the human psyche and the mysteries of the unknown.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: Madness, death, the supernatural, psychological terror
