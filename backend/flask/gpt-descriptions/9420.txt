Synopsis: In "The Hand of Ethelberta," a young and talented woman named Ethelberta Petherwin navigates the complexities of Victorian society as she strives to rise above her humble origins. With her wit, charm, and beauty, Ethelberta captivates the hearts of several suitors, leading to a series of romantic entanglements and unexpected twists. As she balances her aspirations for social advancement with her desire for true love, Ethelberta must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge societal norms.

Genres: Victorian novel, romance, social satire

Themes: Social mobility, gender roles, love and marriage, societal expectations
