Synopsis: In "The Rhyme of the Ancient Mariner," a sailor recounts a harrowing tale of a cursed voyage that brings him face to face with supernatural forces and tests his very soul. As he navigates treacherous waters and encounters eerie creatures, the mariner's story becomes a haunting exploration of guilt, redemption, and the power of nature. 

Genres: Poetry, Gothic literature, Romanticism

Themes: Guilt and redemption, the supernatural, the power of nature, the consequences of human actions
