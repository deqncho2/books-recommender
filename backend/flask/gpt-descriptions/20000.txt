Synopsis: 
Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea takes readers on an extraordinary underwater adventure as they join Professor Aronnax and his companions aboard the enigmatic submarine, the Nautilus. Led by the enigmatic Captain Nemo, they explore the depths of the ocean, encountering breathtaking wonders and terrifying creatures. As they delve deeper into this mysterious world, they are confronted with moral dilemmas and philosophical questions that challenge their understanding of humanity and the natural world. Will they uncover the secrets that lie beneath the sea, or will they be forever lost in its depths?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Nature vs. Technology, Morality, Identity
