Synopsis: In 'Frenzied Fiction' by Stephen Leacock, prepare to be whisked away on a whirlwind adventure through the absurd and hilarious world of fiction. From a mischievous talking parrot to a bumbling detective with a knack for stumbling upon trouble, this collection of short stories will have you laughing out loud and eagerly turning the pages. With Leacock's signature wit and clever storytelling, each tale is a delightful romp that will leave you craving for more.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Absurdity, Irony, Parody
