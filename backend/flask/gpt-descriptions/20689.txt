Synopsis:
'Ragged Dick' follows the life of a young, street-smart boy named Dick Hunter, who navigates the bustling streets of New York City in the mid-19th century. Despite his impoverished background, Dick possesses an unwavering determination to rise above his circumstances and make a name for himself. Through a series of encounters and unexpected opportunities, Dick's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test as he strives to improve his social standing and secure a better future for himself.

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes:
Social mobility, Perseverance, Self-improvement
