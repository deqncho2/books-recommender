Synopsis: In 'Eight Hundred Leagues on the Amazon', a young Brazilian engineer embarks on a perilous journey through the heart of the Amazon rainforest. As he navigates treacherous waters, encounters dangerous wildlife, and faces the threat of hostile tribes, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and adventure. Along the way, he discovers hidden treasures, encounters a captivating young woman, and unravels the secrets of an ancient civilization. This thrilling tale of exploration and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they join the protagonist on his epic quest through the untamed wilderness of the Amazon.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Ancient Civilizations
