Synopsis: 
'The Devil's Dictionary' by Ambrose Bierce is a satirical lexicon that offers witty and sardonic definitions of various words, challenging conventional wisdom and exposing the absurdities of human nature. With a sharp and biting humor, Bierce takes aim at society, politics, religion, and other aspects of human existence, providing a thought-provoking and entertaining commentary on the human condition.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Dictionary

Themes: Satire, Critique of Society, Language and Meaning
