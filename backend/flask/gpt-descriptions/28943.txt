Synopsis:
In 'Dorothy Dale - A Girl of Today' by Margaret Penrose, readers are introduced to the spirited and adventurous Dorothy Dale. Set in a small town, Dorothy finds herself caught up in a series of mysterious events that test her courage and determination. As she unravels the secrets surrounding her new friends and the town itself, Dorothy's resourcefulness and quick thinking become her greatest assets. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth alongside Dorothy.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
