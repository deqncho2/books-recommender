Synopsis: 
In 'Nellie Bly's Book: Around the World in Seventy-Two Days', the fearless and determined journalist Nellie Bly embarks on a groundbreaking journey to circumnavigate the globe in record-breaking time. With only a small bag and her indomitable spirit, she faces numerous challenges, unexpected encounters, and thrilling adventures across continents. As she races against time, Bly's captivating narrative takes readers on a whirlwind expedition, offering a unique glimpse into the diverse cultures, landscapes, and people she encounters along the way.

Genres: 
Adventure, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: 
Courage, Exploration, Gender Equality
