Synopsis: In the late 19th century, Edna Pontellier, a young wife and mother, finds herself trapped in the confines of societal expectations and the limitations of her own existence. As she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Edna begins to question the roles assigned to women and the sacrifices they are expected to make. Through her encounters with unconventional characters and her exploration of her own desires, Edna's awakening becomes a catalyst for personal liberation and a challenge to the norms of her time.

Genres: Fiction, Feminist Literature, Psychological Drama

Themes: Female Identity, Freedom and Conformity, Sexuality and Desire, Societal Expectations
