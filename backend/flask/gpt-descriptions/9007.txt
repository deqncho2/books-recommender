Synopsis: 
Adventures of Huckleberry Finn follows the escapades of a young boy named Huck Finn, who embarks on a thrilling journey down the Mississippi River. Fleeing from his abusive father, Huck teams up with an escaped slave named Jim, and together they navigate the treacherous waters, encountering a colorful cast of characters along the way. From con artists to feuding families, Huck and Jim's adventures are filled with danger, humor, and unexpected twists that keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: 
Racial prejudice, Freedom, Morality, Hypocrisy
