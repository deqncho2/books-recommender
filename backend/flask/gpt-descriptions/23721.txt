Synopsis: 

In 'Chronicles of Canada Volume 6 - The Great Intendant: A Chronicle of Jean Talon in Canada 1665-1672' by Chapais, Thomas, readers are transported to 17th century Canada, where they follow the remarkable journey of Jean Talon, the first Intendant of New France. Talon's arrival in Canada coincides with a pivotal moment in the country's history, as it transitions from a French colony to a thriving settlement. As the Great Intendant, Talon faces numerous challenges and obstacles, from managing the economy and implementing new policies to dealing with conflicts and forging alliances with Indigenous peoples. Through his tireless efforts, Talon leaves an indelible mark on Canada, shaping its future and laying the foundation for its growth and prosperity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Colonialism, Governance, Cultural exchange
