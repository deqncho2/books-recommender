Synopsis: 

'LandSat Picture of Washington, DC' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the iconic city, showcasing stunning satellite images that reveal the hidden beauty and intricate details of Washington, DC. From the majestic monuments to the bustling streets, this book offers a unique perspective that will leave readers in awe of the city's grandeur and complexity. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Photography, Travel

Themes: Urban landscapes, Architectural beauty, Historical significance
