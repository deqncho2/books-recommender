Synopsis: In 'Round the Red Lamp', Arthur Conan Doyle takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark and mysterious world of medical practice. From the eerie atmosphere of the operating theater to the secrets hidden behind closed doors, this collection of thrilling short stories explores the lives of doctors and patients alike, revealing the shocking and often chilling realities of the medical profession. As the characters navigate through life and death situations, unexpected twists and turns keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Medical ethics, Human mortality, The supernatural
