Synopsis: In the dark streets of Victorian London, a respected doctor named Henry Jekyll becomes entangled in a mysterious and sinister experiment. As he delves deeper into his own psyche, he unleashes a terrifying alter ego known as Mr. Hyde. With the city gripped by fear and a series of shocking events unfolding, Jekyll must confront the consequences of his dangerous creation before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Duality of human nature, the struggle between good and evil, the consequences of scientific experimentation
