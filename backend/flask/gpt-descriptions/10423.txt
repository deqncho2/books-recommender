Synopsis: 
In 'Triplets: Fox Trot', three identical sisters find themselves caught in a web of mystery and danger when they stumble upon a hidden secret that threatens their lives and the lives of those around them. As they navigate through a world of deception and betrayal, the triplets must rely on their unique skills and unbreakable bond to unravel the truth and outsmart their adversaries. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Sisterhood, Deception, Betrayal
