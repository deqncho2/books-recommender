Synopsis: 
'At the Moving Picture Ball' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the glamorous world of early Hollywood. As the story unfolds, secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances emerge, all set against the backdrop of a grand ball where the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Deception, Ambition, Identity
