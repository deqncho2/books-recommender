Synopsis: In 'Major Barbara' by Bernard Shaw, a young woman named Barbara, who is a major in the Salvation Army, finds herself torn between her duty to help the poor and her family's wealth and influence. As she navigates the complexities of her personal and professional life, she encounters a charming and enigmatic millionaire who challenges her beliefs and forces her to question the true nature of charity and the power dynamics at play in society. With wit and sharp social commentary, Shaw explores the clash between idealism and pragmatism, wealth and poverty, and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Drama, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Social class, Wealth and poverty, Idealism vs. pragmatism, Power dynamics
