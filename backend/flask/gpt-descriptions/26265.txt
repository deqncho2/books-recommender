Synopsis:
In the final installment of the beloved Anne of Green Gables series, we follow the story of Rilla Blythe, the youngest daughter of Anne Shirley. Set during World War I, Rilla's life takes an unexpected turn as she navigates the challenges and heartaches of war on the home front. With her brothers off to fight, Rilla finds herself facing new responsibilities and discovering her own strength and resilience. As she grows into a young woman, Rilla's journey is filled with love, loss, and the indomitable spirit of hope.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes:
War and its impact on families, Love and sacrifice, Coming of age and self-discovery
