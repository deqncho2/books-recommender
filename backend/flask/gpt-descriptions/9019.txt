Synopsis: 
'A Dog's Tale' by Mark Twain is a heartwarming and thought-provoking novella that follows the life of a loyal and intelligent dog named Aileen. From her early days as a playful puppy to her experiences as a beloved family pet, Aileen's story takes unexpected turns that challenge her unwavering loyalty and reveal the complexities of human nature. Through Aileen's eyes, readers are invited to reflect on the bonds between humans and animals, the power of empathy, and the enduring spirit of love and devotion.

Genres: Fiction, Animal Literature, Satire

Themes: Loyalty, Human-Animal Relationships, Empathy, Love and Devotion
