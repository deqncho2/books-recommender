Synopsis:
In "Dorothy and the Wizard in Oz," Dorothy finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz, but this time she is accompanied by her faithful dog Toto and her new friend Zeb. As they journey through the strange and captivating landscapes of Oz, they encounter a variety of peculiar creatures and face thrilling adventures. Along the way, they must rely on their wit and courage to overcome obstacles and find their way back home.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Exploration
