Synopsis: In a small Austrian village, three young boys encounter a mysterious stranger who possesses an uncanny ability to manipulate reality. As they delve deeper into his enigmatic nature, they are confronted with profound questions about the nature of good and evil, the power of imagination, and the true meaning of existence. With each encounter, the stranger challenges their beliefs and forces them to confront the darker aspects of human nature. As the boys struggle to make sense of the stranger's presence, they are left wondering if he is a divine being or a malevolent force. The truth they uncover will forever change their lives and challenge their perception of the world.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Satire

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Morality, Existentialism, Imagination, Human Nature
