Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Dilemma' by Bernard Shaw, a renowned doctor finds himself torn between his duty to save lives and the moral complexities that arise when he must choose who to save. As he grapples with this ethical dilemma, a group of eccentric artists and intellectuals enter the scene, adding a touch of humor and wit to the story. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking conversations, Shaw explores the boundaries of medicine, love, and the value of human life.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Artistic Integrity, Love and Relationships
