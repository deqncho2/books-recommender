Synopsis: 
In the enchanting tale of 'Rapunzel' by Jacob and Wilhelm Grimm, a young girl with impossibly long hair finds herself locked away in a tower by a wicked enchantress. With only her flowing locks and a mysterious prince as her companions, Rapunzel embarks on a journey of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of freedom. As she navigates the challenges of her secluded life, Rapunzel's resilience and determination shine through, leading her to uncover the truth about her past and the power within her. 

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Freedom, Self-discovery, Love, Resilience
