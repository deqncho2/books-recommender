Synopsis: 
In '20,000 Leagues under the Sea' by Jules Verne, a mysterious and enigmatic sea creature is terrorizing the oceans, causing havoc among sailors and scientists alike. Professor Pierre Aronnax, a renowned marine biologist, is invited to join an expedition to hunt down this creature. Little does he know that this journey will lead him to encounter the enigmatic Captain Nemo and his extraordinary submarine, the Nautilus. As they explore the depths of the ocean, Aronnax and his companions witness breathtaking wonders, face thrilling dangers, and grapple with the moral implications of Nemo's actions. This gripping tale of adventure, exploration, and the mysteries of the deep sea will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Morality, Man vs. Nature
