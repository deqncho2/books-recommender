Synopsis: In 'The Happy Prince, and Other Tales', Oscar Wilde weaves together a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking stories that transport readers to a world where statues come to life, nightingales sing with unmatched beauty, and selfish giants learn the power of compassion. From the tale of a devoted swallow to the story of a remarkable rocket, each narrative is filled with vivid imagery, whimsical characters, and profound insights into the human condition. As the pages turn, readers will be captivated by the magic and wonder that emanate from Wilde's words, leaving them with a renewed appreciation for the beauty and kindness that can be found in even the most unexpected places.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Short stories

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Beauty and art, Social criticism, Compassion and empathy
