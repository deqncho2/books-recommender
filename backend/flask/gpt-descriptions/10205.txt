Synopsis: In 'Words of Welcome: Opening of the Electrical Exposition of New York City', author Garret A. Hobart takes readers on a captivating journey through the grand opening of a groundbreaking electrical exposition in the heart of New York City. As the event unfolds, the reader is immersed in a world of technological marvels, awe-inspiring displays, and the electrifying atmosphere of progress and innovation. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Hobart paints a vivid picture of this monumental event, leaving readers eager to discover the wonders that await within the exposition's walls.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Technological advancement, Innovation, Progress
