Synopsis: In 'What Men Live By and Other Tales', Tolstoy weaves together a collection of captivating and thought-provoking stories that explore the depths of human nature and the mysteries of life. From a humble shoemaker who discovers the true meaning of love, to a wealthy landowner who embarks on a journey of self-discovery, each tale presents a unique and profound insight into the human condition. With unexpected twists and poignant moments, these stories will leave readers pondering the essence of existence and the power of compassion.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Redemption, Morality, Spiritual Awakening
