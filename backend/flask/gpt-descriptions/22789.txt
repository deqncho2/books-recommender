Synopsis: 
In 'On the Duties of the Clergy', Saint Ambrose, the influential Bishop of Milan, offers a compelling and thought-provoking guide for clergy members. Through a series of letters, he explores the challenges and responsibilities faced by those in religious leadership, delving into topics such as moral conduct, humility, and the pursuit of justice. With his insightful and practical advice, Ambrose provides a timeless resource for clergy members seeking to navigate their roles with integrity and purpose.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Epistolary

Themes: Moral conduct, Humility, Justice, Religious leadership
