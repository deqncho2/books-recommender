Synopsis: Step into the dazzling world of innovation and marvel at the wonders of electricity in 'Electricity and Progress: Opening of the New York Electrical Show' by Thomas A. Edison. Join the author as he takes you on a captivating journey through the grand exhibition, where cutting-edge electrical inventions and breathtaking displays await. From the mesmerizing glow of incandescent bulbs to the awe-inspiring power of electric motors, this immersive experience will leave you in awe of the boundless potential of electricity. Prepare to be enlightened and inspired by the remarkable advancements that are shaping the future of our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Technological progress, Innovation, Scientific discovery
