Synopsis: In 'Life's Little Ironies: A set of tales with some colloquial sketches entitled A Few Crusted Characters', Thomas Hardy weaves together a collection of captivating stories that explore the complexities of human nature and the ironic twists of fate. From the humorous encounters of eccentric characters to the poignant struggles of individuals trapped in societal expectations, these tales offer a glimpse into the unpredictable and often contradictory nature of life. With Hardy's masterful storytelling, readers are taken on a journey filled with unexpected surprises and thought-provoking insights.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Satire

Themes: Irony, Fate, Society, Human Nature
