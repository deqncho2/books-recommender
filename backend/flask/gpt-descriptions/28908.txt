Synopsis: In "A Dream within a Dream," Edgar Allan Poe takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the enigmatic realms of dreams and reality. The narrator grapples with the ephemeral nature of existence, questioning the boundaries between what is real and what is merely an illusion. As the lines blur, the reader is left pondering the profound mysteries of life and the fleeting nature of time.

Genres: Poetry, Dark Romanticism

Themes: Illusion vs. Reality, Transience of Time, Existentialism
