Synopsis: In "The Light Princess" by George MacDonald, a young princess is cursed with weightlessness, causing her to float above the ground. As she grows up, she becomes a carefree and mischievous spirit, unaffected by the troubles of the world. However, when she meets a prince from a neighboring kingdom, her life takes an unexpected turn. Together, they embark on a whimsical and enchanting journey filled with adventure, love, and self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Romance

Themes: Freedom and Confinement, Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-acceptance
