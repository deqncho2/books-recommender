Synopsis: In 'The Rocket Book' by Peter Newell, embark on an exhilarating adventure as a mischievous young boy discovers a rocket and sets off on an unexpected journey. As the rocket takes off, it leads him through a series of comical and unpredictable events, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. Will the boy be able to control the rocket and find his way back home?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Imagination, Curiosity, Exploration
