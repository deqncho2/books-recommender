Synopsis: 

"The Survivors of the Chancellor: Diary of J.R. Kazallon, Passenger" by Jules Verne is a gripping tale that follows the harrowing journey of a group of passengers aboard the ill-fated Chancellor ship. Through the eyes of J.R. Kazallon, a young man with a mysterious past, we witness the escalating tensions, desperate struggles, and moral dilemmas faced by the survivors as they battle against the merciless forces of nature and their own inner demons. As the ship drifts aimlessly across the vast expanse of the ocean, the passengers must confront their deepest fears and question the limits of human endurance. 

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Morality, Desperation, Human Resilience
