Synopsis: In "On the Antiseptic Principle of the Practice of Surgery," Joseph Lister, Baron takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of surgical practices. Through meticulous observations and groundbreaking experiments, Lister unveils a revolutionary method that could potentially transform the field of surgery forever. As he battles skepticism and resistance, Lister's determination to improve patient outcomes and prevent infections becomes a gripping tale of scientific discovery and medical innovation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical literature, Scientific literature

Themes: Medical advancements, Scientific discovery, Innovation, Patient care
