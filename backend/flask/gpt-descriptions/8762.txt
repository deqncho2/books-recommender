Synopsis:
In the medieval era of England, a mysterious and feared figure known as The Outlaw of Torn emerges. This enigmatic character, whose true identity remains shrouded in secrecy, becomes a symbol of rebellion against the oppressive ruling class. As he navigates through a treacherous world of political intrigue, power struggles, and forbidden love, The Outlaw of Torn must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape the destiny of a nation.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
Rebellion, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
