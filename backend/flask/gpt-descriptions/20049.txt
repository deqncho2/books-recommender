Synopsis: 
'Great Big Treasury of Beatrix Potter' is a delightful collection of beloved children's stories written and illustrated by the talented Beatrix Potter. This enchanting treasury features classic tales such as "The Tale of Peter Rabbit," "The Tale of Benjamin Bunny," and "The Tale of Jemima Puddle-Duck," among others. Each story takes young readers on a whimsical journey through the charming world of animals, filled with adventure, mischief, and valuable life lessons. From mischievous rabbits to clever ducks, this collection is sure to captivate both children and adults alike with its timeless tales and beautiful illustrations.

Genres: Children's literature, Picture book, Fantasy

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Morality, Nature
