Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Dunnet Landing, a young writer arrives seeking solitude and inspiration. As she immerses herself in the lives of the local inhabitants, she uncovers a tapestry of stories and experiences that reveal the true essence of this close-knit community. Through her encounters with strong-willed women, wise fishermen, and eccentric individuals, she discovers the beauty and resilience of the human spirit, and the profound connections that can be forged in unexpected places.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Regionalism

Themes: Community, Connection, Resilience, Femininity, Nature
