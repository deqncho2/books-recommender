Synopsis: In 'The Man Who was Thursday, A Nightmare', Gabriel Syme, a poet and undercover detective, is recruited by a secret anti-anarchist organization. As he delves deeper into the mysterious world of anarchists, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, deception, and surreal encounters. With each twist and turn, Syme's perception of reality is challenged, leading him to question the true nature of his mission and the identities of those around him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Satire

Themes: Anarchy vs. Order, Identity, Perception of Reality
