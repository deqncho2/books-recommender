Synopsis: In 'History of the United States, Volume 2: Conflict & Independence', Charles A. Beard and Mary Ritter Beard delve into the tumultuous period of American history from the Revolutionary War to the early years of the nation's independence. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, the authors bring to life the struggles, conflicts, and triumphs that shaped the birth of a new nation. From the battles of Lexington and Concord to the signing of the Declaration of Independence, this volume offers a comprehensive exploration of the events and individuals that defined America's fight for freedom.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: American Revolution, Independence, Founding Fathers, Revolutionary War, Nation-building
