Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Moonlight Bay, strange occurrences begin to unfold, leaving the residents bewildered and fearful. As the mystery deepens, a young woman named Emily finds herself at the center of it all, uncovering dark secrets that have long been buried. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she must navigate through a web of deceit, supernatural forces, and personal demons to unravel the truth and save her town from impending doom.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, the power of the unknown, the resilience of the human spirit
