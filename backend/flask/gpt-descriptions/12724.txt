Synopsis: 
In 'Sonnet #116' by William Shakespeare, the speaker passionately explores the nature of true love, challenging conventional notions and emphasizing its enduring power. Through vivid imagery and thought-provoking metaphors, the sonnet delves into the complexities of love, its ability to withstand the test of time, and its unwavering constancy. The speaker's words resonate with a profound sense of truth and wisdom, inviting readers to reflect on the essence of love and its profound impact on human relationships.

Genres: Poetry, Sonnet

Themes: Love, Time, Constancy
