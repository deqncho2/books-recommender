Synopsis: Two Rubes at the Vaudeville is a hilarious and heartwarming tale that follows the misadventures of two small-town friends who find themselves thrust into the glamorous world of vaudeville. As they navigate the challenges and eccentricities of the entertainment industry, their friendship is put to the test, and they must learn to embrace their true selves amidst the glitz and glamour. With a delightful blend of comedy, romance, and unexpected twists, this story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Pursuing Dreams
