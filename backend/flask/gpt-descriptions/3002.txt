Synopsis: 
In "Society's Child (audiofile)," a young woman named Lily finds herself caught between the expectations of her conservative family and her own desires for love and acceptance. Set in the backdrop of the 1960s, Lily's journey takes her through a tumultuous period of social change, where she must navigate the complexities of race, class, and gender. As she grapples with the consequences of her choices, Lily discovers the power of self-discovery and the strength to challenge societal norms.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Prejudice, Rebellion, Love, Social Change
