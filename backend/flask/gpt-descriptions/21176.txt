Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Three Little Pigs' by Brooke, L. Leslie, three little pigs set out to build their own houses, each with a different material. However, a cunning wolf is determined to blow their houses down. Will the pigs be able to outsmart the wolf and protect their homes?

Genres: Children's literature, Fairy tale, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Resourcefulness, Problem-solving
