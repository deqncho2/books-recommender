Synopsis: In the eerie moors of Dartmoor, a curse haunts the Baskerville family, claiming the lives of its heirs. When Sir Charles Baskerville is found dead under mysterious circumstances, his friend Dr. John Watson and the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes are called upon to solve the chilling case. As they delve into the secrets of the Baskerville estate, they encounter a terrifying legend of a demonic hound that prowls the night, ready to strike again. With time running out, Holmes and Watson must unravel the truth behind the curse before it claims its next victim.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: The supernatural, the power of fear, the conflict between reason and superstition
