Synopsis: In the heart of medieval Paris, a deformed and lonely bell-ringer named Quasimodo finds solace in the grand cathedral of Notre Dame. As he navigates a world filled with cruelty and prejudice, Quasimodo's life becomes intertwined with that of the beautiful gypsy dancer, Esmeralda, and the charismatic but morally conflicted Archdeacon, Claude Frollo. Amidst a backdrop of political turmoil and forbidden desires, the fate of these three individuals becomes entangled in a tale of love, betrayal, and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Gothic Literature

Themes: Beauty and ugliness, social injustice, the power of love and compassion, the struggle between good and evil
