Synopsis: 
In 'Time's Laughingstocks and Other Verses', Thomas Hardy takes readers on a poetic journey through the complexities of life, love, and the passage of time. From poignant reflections on lost love to contemplations on the fleeting nature of happiness, Hardy's verses capture the essence of human emotions and the bittersweet beauty of existence. With his signature lyrical style and profound observations, he delves into the depths of the human soul, exploring themes of love, loss, mortality, and the inevitable changes that shape our lives.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Time, Mortality, Human Emotions
