Synopsis: In "Is Shakespeare Dead?" Mark Twain embarks on a witty and thought-provoking investigation into the true identity of William Shakespeare, challenging the widely accepted narrative surrounding the legendary playwright. With his signature humor and sharp intellect, Twain delves into the historical evidence, questioning the authenticity of Shakespeare's works and exploring the possibility of an imposter behind the famous name. As he unravels the mysteries surrounding Shakespeare's life, Twain offers a captivating and unconventional perspective on one of literature's greatest enigmas.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Identity, Authorship, Historical Accuracy
