Synopsis: In 'Off on a Comet' by Jules Verne, a small group of people find themselves stranded on a comet that has collided with Earth. As they navigate this strange new world, they encounter bizarre landscapes, encounter other survivors, and face unexpected challenges. With their lives forever changed, they must find a way to adapt and survive in this extraordinary situation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Adaptation
