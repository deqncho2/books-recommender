Synopsis: In 'The Frog Prince and Other Stories' by Walter Crane, readers will embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales. From the classic story of a prince transformed into a frog to the adventures of a brave little girl and her magical encounters, these timeless narratives will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and imagination. With vibrant illustrations and engaging storytelling, Crane's collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy tales, Children's literature, Fantasy

Themes: Transformation, Courage, Magic, Love
