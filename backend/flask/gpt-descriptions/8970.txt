Synopsis:
In "Twelve Stories and a Dream," H.G. Wells takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of twelve imaginative and thought-provoking tales. From a man who discovers a mysterious ability to control time to a scientist who invents a machine that can alter human personality, each story presents a unique and unexpected twist. As the characters navigate through their extraordinary experiences, they grapple with the consequences of their actions and the boundaries of human potential. With Wells' signature blend of science fiction, fantasy, and social commentary, "Twelve Stories and a Dream" offers a captivating exploration of the human condition and the power of the imagination.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Social Commentary

Themes: Time, Human Potential, Consequences of Actions, Imagination
