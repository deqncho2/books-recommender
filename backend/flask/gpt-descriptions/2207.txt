Synopsis: In 'Human Genome Project, rough draft, Chromosome Number 07', the Human Genome Project takes an unexpected turn as scientists uncover a hidden code within the seventh chromosome. As they delve deeper into this mysterious genetic sequence, they stumble upon a shocking revelation that could change the course of human evolution forever. With the clock ticking, a team of brilliant researchers must race against time to unlock the secrets hidden within this enigmatic chromosome before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Genetic manipulation, Ethical dilemmas, Scientific discovery
