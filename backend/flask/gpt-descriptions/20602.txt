Synopsis: In 'Cobb's Anatomy', Irvin S. Cobb takes us on a captivating journey through the human body, exploring its intricate workings and revealing fascinating anecdotes along the way. From the curious origins of certain body parts to the peculiarities of our senses, Cobb's witty and insightful observations shed light on the marvels and mysteries of our own anatomy. With a blend of humor, scientific knowledge, and storytelling prowess, this book offers a delightful exploration of the human body that will leave readers both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Humor

Themes: Human anatomy, Curiosity, Wonder
