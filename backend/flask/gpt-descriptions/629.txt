Synopsis: In "The Square Root of 5," a young mathematician named David embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love as he navigates the complexities of his personal and professional life. As he delves deeper into the world of numbers and equations, David finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected connections that challenge his perception of reality. With the help of an enigmatic mentor and a mysterious woman, David must confront his fears and confront the truth that lies beneath the surface. This captivating novel explores the intricate balance between logic and emotion, and the profound impact that love and knowledge can have on one's life.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Perception, Truth, Knowledge
