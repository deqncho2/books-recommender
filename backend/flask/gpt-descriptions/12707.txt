Synopsis: Four talented animals, a donkey, a dog, a cat, and a rooster, embark on a thrilling adventure to become musicians. Along the way, they encounter a mysterious house filled with robbers and must use their wit and musical talents to outsmart them. Will their dreams of becoming musicians come true, or will they be forever silenced?

Genres: Fairy tale, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Friendship, Resourcefulness
