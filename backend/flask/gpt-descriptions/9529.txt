Synopsis: In "The History of Samuel Titmarsh and the Great Hoggarty Diamond," we follow the misadventures of Samuel Titmarsh, a young artist who finds himself entangled in the extravagant and often absurd world of the Hoggarty family. As he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and social status, Samuel becomes an unwitting witness to the family's obsession with a magnificent diamond. With wit and satire, Thackeray takes us on a humorous and insightful journey through the follies and foibles of Victorian society.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Victorian Fiction

Themes: Social class, Materialism, Love and relationships
