Synopsis: In 'What Band Is This?' by Hann's Emperors of Song, a mysterious band emerges from the shadows, captivating audiences with their enigmatic performances. As music enthusiasts and journalists scramble to uncover the identity of the band members, they become entangled in a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Music

Themes: Identity, Obsession, Betrayal
