Synopsis: John Carter, a former Confederate soldier, mysteriously finds himself transported to the planet Mars, known as Barsoom to its inhabitants. In this strange new world, Carter discovers a land filled with towering green warriors, fierce creatures, and captivating princesses. As he navigates the treacherous Martian landscape, Carter becomes embroiled in a conflict between rival Martian tribes and must use his newfound strength and skills to survive. Along the way, he finds love, faces betrayal, and uncovers the secrets of his extraordinary journey.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Heroism, Love and Sacrifice
