Synopsis: In 'The Railroad Question' by William Jennings Bryan, a small town is thrown into chaos when a controversial railroad project threatens to disrupt their way of life. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the townspeople must navigate a web of political intrigue, personal ambitions, and moral dilemmas. With unexpected twists and a cast of compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the consequences of progress and the power of community.

Genres: Fiction, Political Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Progress vs. Tradition, Power and Corruption, Community and Unity
