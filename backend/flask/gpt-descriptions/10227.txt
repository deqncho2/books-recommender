Synopsis: In 'Tolstoy's Plower Story', a young man named Ivan embarks on a journey of self-discovery and enlightenment after a chance encounter with a wise old plower. As Ivan delves deeper into the plower's teachings, he begins to question his own beliefs and the meaning of life. Set against the backdrop of the Russian countryside, this thought-provoking tale explores the complexities of human existence and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Philosophical, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Enlightenment, Existentialism
