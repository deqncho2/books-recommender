Synopsis: 
When Matthew Cuthbert decides to adopt a boy to help him and his sister Marilla on their farm, he is in for a surprise. Instead of a boy, he is sent a spirited and imaginative red-haired girl named Anne Shirley. Despite the initial mix-up, Anne quickly finds her place in the hearts of the Cuthberts and the community of Avonlea, with her infectious enthusiasm and vivid imagination. Follow Anne's journey as she navigates the ups and downs of life, makes lasting friendships, and discovers the true meaning of family and belonging in the charming world of Green Gables.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Classic

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Belonging, Family
