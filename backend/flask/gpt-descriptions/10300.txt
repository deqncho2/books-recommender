Synopsis: 
In 'Snyder, Does Your Mother Know You're Out?', a small town is shaken by the sudden disappearance of a young boy named Snyder. As the community rallies together to search for him, dark secrets and hidden truths begin to unravel, revealing a web of deceit and betrayal. With each revelation, the mystery deepens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, desperate to uncover the truth behind Snyder's disappearance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Community, Truth
