Synopsis: 
In 'Roundabout Papers', Thackeray takes us on a delightful journey through his witty and observant musings on various topics of Victorian society. From the quirks of everyday life to the follies of the upper class, he offers a satirical and insightful perspective that will keep readers captivated. Through a series of essays and sketches, Thackeray's keen eye for detail and his ability to find humor in the mundane will leave readers both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Satire, Essay, Humor

Themes: Victorian society, Social satire, Human nature
