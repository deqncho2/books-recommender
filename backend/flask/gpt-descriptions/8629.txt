Synopsis: 
In "Sir Nigel" by Arthur Conan Doyle, we follow the captivating journey of a young knight named Nigel Loring during the tumultuous times of the Hundred Years' War. Filled with thrilling battles, daring quests, and unexpected alliances, Nigel's quest for honor and love takes him from the grandeur of the English court to the treacherous battlefields of France. As he faces formidable foes and navigates the complexities of chivalry, Nigel must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Love, Loyalty
