Synopsis: In this captivating recording from the early days of cinema, Thomas Edison brings to life the beloved nursery rhyme "Mary Had a Little Lamb" in a heartwarming and enchanting way. Through the magic of Movietone Production, viewers are transported to a world where the innocence and joy of childhood are beautifully captured. As the story unfolds, audiences will be delighted by the charming characters and the unexpected twists that make this rendition of the classic tale truly unforgettable.

Genres: Short film, Musical, Family

Themes: Childhood innocence, Friendship, Imagination
