Synopsis: 
'Interruptions: Comic Sketch' by Woottwell, Tom is a collection of hilarious and unexpected comic sketches that will keep readers laughing from start to finish. From absurd situations to clever wordplay, each sketch presents a unique interruption that takes the reader on a delightful rollercoaster of comedic surprises. Whether it's a mundane conversation turned upside down or a simple task gone awry, these interruptions will leave readers eagerly turning the pages to see what unexpected twist awaits next.

Genres: Comedy, Humor, Satire

Themes: Absurdity, Wordplay, Unexpected Surprises
