Synopsis: 
In 'The Constitution of the United States of America, 1787', the United States embarks on a groundbreaking journey to establish a framework for a new nation. As delegates from different states gather in Philadelphia, they face immense challenges and conflicting visions for the future. Through intense debates and compromises, they strive to create a document that will shape the destiny of a nation and safeguard the rights of its citizens. This gripping account unveils the struggles, triumphs, and complexities behind the creation of one of the most influential documents in history.

Genres: Historical, Political, Legal

Themes: Democracy, Individual Rights, Governance, Equality, Compromise
