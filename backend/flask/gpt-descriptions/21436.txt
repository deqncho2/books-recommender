Synopsis: 
'Poems Every Child Should Know' is a delightful collection of classic poems that will transport readers of all ages into a world of imagination and wonder. From the enchanting verses of Robert Frost to the whimsical rhymes of Lewis Carroll, this anthology offers a diverse range of poems that will captivate and inspire. Whether it's the stirring words of Emily Dickinson or the playful rhythms of Edward Lear, each poem in this collection is carefully selected to ignite a love for poetry in every child's heart. With themes ranging from nature and adventure to love and friendship, 'Poems Every Child Should Know' is a timeless treasure that will leave readers eager to explore the beauty of language and the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature, Anthology
Themes: Imagination, Nature, Adventure, Love, Friendship
