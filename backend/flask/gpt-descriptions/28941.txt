Synopsis: In 'The Princess and the Goblin' by George MacDonald, a young princess named Irene discovers a secret world beneath her castle, inhabited by mysterious creatures. With the help of a brave miner boy named Curdie, Irene embarks on a perilous adventure to save her kingdom from an ancient evil. As they face treacherous goblins and unravel the secrets of their underground realm, Irene and Curdie learn the power of courage, friendship, and the importance of believing in the unseen.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Fairy Tale

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Good vs. Evil, Belief in the Unseen
