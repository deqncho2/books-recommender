Synopsis: In 'Bad Medicine' by Robert Sheckley, a renowned doctor finds himself in a peculiar situation when he is transported to a distant planet to treat an alien patient. However, he soon discovers that the medical practices on this planet are far from what he is accustomed to, leading him on a thrilling and mind-bending journey to uncover the truth behind this strange world. As he navigates through bizarre customs and encounters peculiar beings, the doctor must question his own beliefs and confront the ethical dilemmas that arise. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Cultural relativism, Ethical dilemmas, Perception of reality
