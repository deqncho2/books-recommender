Synopsis: 
In "One Divided by Pi (To 1 Million Digits)" by Kanada, Yasumasa, embark on a mind-bending journey into the depths of mathematical exploration. This captivating work takes readers on a quest to unravel the mysteries hidden within the infinite digits of the mathematical constant, pi. As the author delves into the intricacies of this enigmatic number, unexpected connections and patterns emerge, challenging conventional notions of mathematics and inspiring a profound sense of wonder.

Genres: 
Mathematics, Non-fiction, Science

Themes: 
Infinity, Mathematical exploration, Patterns
