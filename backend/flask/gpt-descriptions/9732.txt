Synopsis: In the midst of political unrest and social upheaval, a mysterious murder sets off a chain of events that will test the bonds of love, loyalty, and friendship. Set against the backdrop of the Gordon Riots in 18th-century London, 'Barnaby Rudge' follows the lives of a diverse cast of characters as they navigate through a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and the pursuit of justice. As the tension escalates and the city descends into chaos, the true nature of humanity is revealed, leaving the reader questioning the limits of compassion and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Social Commentary

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Prejudice, Mob Mentality, Love and Sacrifice
