Synopsis:
In "Master and Man" by Leo Tolstoy, a wealthy landowner named Vasili Andreevich sets out on a treacherous journey through a snowstorm with his loyal servant, Nikita. As they navigate the harsh winter landscape, their contrasting personalities and motivations are put to the test. With their lives hanging in the balance, the master and servant must confront their deepest fears and reevaluate their priorities. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Novella, Adventure

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Self-discovery
