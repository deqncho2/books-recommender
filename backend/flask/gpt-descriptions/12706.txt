Synopsis: In "The Mouse, the Bird and the Sausage," three unlikely companions embark on a peculiar journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As they navigate through their daily tasks, their lives become intertwined in a way that challenges their understanding of friendship and the roles they play. This enchanting tale explores the consequences of breaking societal norms and the importance of embracing one's true nature.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Acceptance
