Synopsis: 
In the early 20th century, a young immigrant named Jurgis Rudkus arrives in Chicago with dreams of a better life. However, he soon discovers that the city's meatpacking industry is a brutal and unforgiving world. As Jurgis navigates the harsh realities of poverty, corruption, and exploitation, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and despair. Will he be able to overcome the challenges and find hope in the midst of this urban jungle?

Genres: 
Social realism, political fiction, immigrant literature

Themes: 
Capitalism and its effects on the working class, corruption and greed, the American Dream, social inequality, the plight of immigrants
