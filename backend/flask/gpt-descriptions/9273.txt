Synopsis: In 'A Modest Proposal', Jonathan Swift presents a unique and audacious solution to a pressing social issue that will leave readers both shocked and intrigued. Through a satirical lens, Swift proposes a controversial idea that challenges societal norms and forces readers to question their own beliefs. This thought-provoking work is sure to captivate and engage readers from start to finish.

Genres: Satire, Political Fiction

Themes: Social criticism, Irony, Morality, Poverty
