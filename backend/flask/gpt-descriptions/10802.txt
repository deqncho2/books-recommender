Synopsis: In 'Project Gutenberg "10K" DVD', a mysterious DVD containing a hidden message falls into the hands of a talented hacker, leading him on a thrilling journey filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of the DVD, he discovers a conspiracy that could change the course of history. With time running out, he must decipher the cryptic clues and outsmart his adversaries before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Technology, Conspiracy, Redemption
