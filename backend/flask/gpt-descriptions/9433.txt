Synopsis: In "Tess of the d'Urbervilles: A Pure Woman," Thomas Hardy weaves a captivating tale of a young woman named Tess who is thrust into a world of love, betrayal, and societal expectations. Set in rural England, Tess's journey takes her through the complexities of class divisions, the struggle for independence, and the consequences of one's past. As she navigates through the challenges of her circumstances, Tess's resilience and determination shine through, making her a truly unforgettable character.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Tragedy

Themes: Social class, Feminism, Morality, Fate
