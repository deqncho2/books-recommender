Synopsis: In 'The Colors of Space' by Marion Zimmer Bradley, a young girl named Bart Steele embarks on a thrilling interstellar journey to uncover the secrets of the mysterious Lhari aliens. With her father's life hanging in the balance, Bart must navigate treacherous space travel, encounter strange new worlds, and confront her own fears and limitations. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic colors of space, Bart discovers that the universe holds more surprises than she could have ever imagined.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Overcoming Fear
