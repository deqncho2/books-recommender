Synopsis: In 'Human Genome Project, Chromosome Number 05', the Human Genome Project takes an unexpected turn when a team of scientists stumbles upon a mysterious anomaly within chromosome number 05. As they delve deeper into the secrets hidden within this particular chromosome, they uncover a shocking revelation that could change the course of human evolution forever. With time running out, the team must race against the clock to unlock the truth before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Genetic manipulation, Ethical dilemmas, Scientific discovery
