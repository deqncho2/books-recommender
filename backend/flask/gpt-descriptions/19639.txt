Synopsis: In the ancient kingdom of Persia, a young Jewish woman named Esther finds herself thrust into a world of political intrigue and danger when she is chosen to be the queen. As she navigates the treacherous court of King Xerxes, Esther must conceal her true identity and use her wit and courage to protect her people from a sinister plot. With high stakes and unexpected twists, 'Book of Esther' is a captivating tale of power, identity, and the strength of one woman's resolve.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Courage, Power, Loyalty
