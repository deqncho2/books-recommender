Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Peter Mink' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the thrilling adventures of Peter Mink, a clever and mischievous little creature who finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of survival. As Peter navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a variety of cunning foes, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to outsmart his enemies and protect his loved ones. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Animal Fiction

Themes: Survival, Courage, Loyalty
