Synopsis: In "Little Men" by Louisa May Alcott, readers are transported back to the beloved world of the March family. This time, the focus is on Jo March's school for boys, Plumfield, where a diverse group of young minds come together to learn and grow. As the boys navigate the challenges of friendship, mischief, and self-discovery, they encounter a host of colorful characters and embark on unforgettable adventures. With its heartwarming storytelling and timeless lessons, "Little Men" is a delightful continuation of the March family saga.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Education, Friendship, Individuality, Moral development
