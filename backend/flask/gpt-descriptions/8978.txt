Synopsis: In "When the Sleeper Wakes," a man falls into a deep sleep only to awaken in a future world where he is hailed as a messiah-like figure. As he navigates this unfamiliar society, he uncovers dark secrets and faces a choice that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science fiction, dystopian fiction, adventure

Themes: Power and control, societal inequality, the consequences of technology
