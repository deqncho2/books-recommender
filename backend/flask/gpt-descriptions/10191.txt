Synopsis: In 'Fourth of July at Punkin Center' by Stewart, Cal, a small town in the heart of America prepares for its annual Fourth of July celebration. As the townspeople come together to plan the festivities, secrets, rivalries, and unexpected events unfold, revealing the true spirit of community and the power of unity. With colorful characters and a touch of humor, this heartwarming tale captures the essence of small-town life and the magic of a holiday celebration.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Contemporary

Themes: Community, Unity, Celebration
