Synopsis: In this enchanting fairy tale, a miller's daughter finds herself in a desperate situation when she is tasked with spinning straw into gold. Just as all hope seems lost, a mysterious little man appears, offering his assistance. But his help comes at a steep price, and the consequences of their agreement may be more than the young girl bargained for. With twists and turns, secrets and magic, "Rumpelstiltskin" is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy

Themes: Power of words, Deception, Redemption
