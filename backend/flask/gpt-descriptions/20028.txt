Synopsis: 
Fanny Hill: Memoirs of a Woman of Pleasure is a provocative and scandalous novel that follows the journey of a young woman named Fanny Hill. Abandoned by her parents, Fanny finds herself thrust into the world of pleasure and desire, as she navigates the seductive and often dangerous realm of 18th-century London. From her first encounter with a wealthy nobleman to her experiences in a high-class brothel, Fanny's story is a captivating exploration of passion, love, and the pursuit of pleasure.

Genres: Erotic fiction, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Sexuality, Morality, Freedom, Social class
