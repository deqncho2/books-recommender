Synopsis: In "Tarzan the Untamed," Edgar Rice Burroughs takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as Tarzan, the legendary ape-man, finds himself in the midst of World War I. When his beloved Jane is kidnapped by a ruthless German officer, Tarzan embarks on a daring mission to rescue her, facing dangerous enemies and untamed wilderness along the way. As the war rages on, Tarzan's strength, cunning, and primal instincts are put to the ultimate test, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and devotion, Survival, Conflict and war
