Synopsis: 
'Film: Set of 4 Atomic Bomb Test Films' takes viewers on a captivating journey through four groundbreaking atomic bomb tests conducted during the mid-20th century. This collection of rare footage showcases the immense power and destructive force of these tests, while also shedding light on the scientific advancements and ethical dilemmas surrounding nuclear weapons. As each test unfolds, the audience is left in awe of the sheer magnitude of these explosions and the profound impact they had on the world. 

Genres: Documentary, Historical, Science

Themes: Nuclear weapons, Scientific advancements, Ethical dilemmas
