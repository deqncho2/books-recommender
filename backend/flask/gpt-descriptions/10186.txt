Synopsis: In "Schultz at the Paris Exposition" by Kennedy, Frank, a young artist named Schultz finds himself immersed in the vibrant world of the Paris Exposition of 1900. As he navigates through the grand exhibition, he encounters a mysterious woman who captivates his imagination and leads him on a journey of self-discovery. With the backdrop of the dazzling Exposition, Schultz's artistic talents are put to the test, and he must confront his deepest desires and fears. This enchanting tale takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of love, art, and the transformative power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Artistic expression, Self-discovery, Love and passion
