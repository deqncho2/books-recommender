Synopsis: In the picturesque Isle of Slingers, a renowned sculptor named Jocelyn Pierston becomes infatuated with three generations of women from the same family. As he searches for his ideal woman, he finds himself torn between love and desire, beauty and imperfection. Spanning several decades, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the elusive nature of true love.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Beauty and imperfection, Relationships and attraction
