Synopsis: In 'Little Wars', H.G. Wells invites readers into a captivating world where boys and girls can engage in an extraordinary game that transcends age and gender. This unique game, designed for the more intellectually inclined, takes players on a thrilling journey through battles, strategy, and imagination. As the game unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, friendships are tested, and the boundaries between reality and make-believe blur. With its clever twists and turns, 'Little Wars' promises to captivate readers of all ages and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Strategy
