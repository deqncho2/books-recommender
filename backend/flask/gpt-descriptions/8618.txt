Synopsis: In 'The Parasite' by Arthur Conan Doyle, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and manipulation. As he becomes increasingly aware of the sinister forces at play, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. With unexpected twists and a sense of impending danger, this gripping story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Loyalty, Identity
