Synopsis: In "A Candle in the Wind" by Mary Imlay Taylor, a young woman finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous conspiracy that threatens to unravel her world. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she must navigate a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her life hanging in the balance, she must uncover the truth before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Secrets, Survival
