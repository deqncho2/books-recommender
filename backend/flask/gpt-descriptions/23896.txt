Synopsis: In the heart of London, a group of anarchists plot a sinister act of terrorism. At the center of this web of intrigue is Adolf Verloc, a seemingly ordinary man leading a double life as a secret agent. As the plan unfolds, Verloc finds himself torn between loyalty to his comrades and the safety of his family. With tension mounting and the threat of violence looming, Verloc must navigate a treacherous world where trust is scarce and betrayal is always just around the corner.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Political fiction, Crime fiction

Themes: Terrorism, Betrayal, Loyalty, Morality
