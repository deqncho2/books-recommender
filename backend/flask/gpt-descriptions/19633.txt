Synopsis: In the epic poem 'Beowulf', a fearless warrior named Beowulf embarks on a perilous journey to rid a kingdom of a terrifying monster. As he battles against supernatural creatures and faces his own mortality, Beowulf's heroic deeds and unwavering determination captivate the reader, leading to a climactic showdown that will test his strength and courage. 

Genres: Epic, Heroic Poetry, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Fate and Destiny, Good vs. Evil
