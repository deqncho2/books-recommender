Synopsis: In the heart of the bustling city, a talented jazz musician finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of his life forever. As he navigates the gritty streets of New York, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and resilience. With the sultry sounds of jazz as the backdrop, 'New York Blues' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of the city, where passion, betrayal, and redemption collide.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Jazz

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Betrayal
