Synopsis: In the whimsical world of 'Jabberwocky' by Lewis Carroll, a young adventurer embarks on a quest to confront a fearsome creature that lurks in the shadows. Along the way, the protagonist encounters a host of peculiar characters and navigates through a topsy-turvy landscape filled with nonsensical language and absurd situations. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a delightful journey that challenges conventional logic and invites them to embrace the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Poetry

Themes: Imagination, Language, Heroism
