Synopsis: 
"The Fall of the House of Usher" follows an unnamed narrator who visits his childhood friend, Roderick Usher, in his decaying ancestral home. As the narrator delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Usher family, he becomes entangled in a web of madness, terror, and supernatural occurrences. The eerie atmosphere and the haunting secrets of the Usher family gradually unravel, leading to a chilling climax that will leave readers questioning reality and the power of the mind.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, psychological thriller

Themes: Madness, decay, isolation, the power of the mind
