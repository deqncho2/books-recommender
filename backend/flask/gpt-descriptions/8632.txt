Synopsis: 
When Lady Frances Carfax mysteriously vanishes while traveling through Europe, her concerned friends turn to the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes for help. As Holmes delves into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and danger that leads him on a thrilling chase across the continent. With each clue he uncovers, Holmes gets closer to unraveling the truth behind Lady Frances Carfax's disappearance, but will he be able to find her before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Disappearance, Deception, Investigation, Loyalty
