Synopsis: 
In 'Ten Days in a Mad-house', Nellie Bly, a fearless and determined journalist, embarks on a daring undercover mission to expose the shocking conditions within a notorious mental institution. Posing as a mentally ill patient, she endures unimaginable hardships and witnesses the cruel treatment inflicted upon the vulnerable inmates. As she navigates the treacherous world of the asylum, Nellie's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning the very nature of sanity and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Biography, Investigative Journalism, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Mental health, Institutional abuse, Advocacy for the vulnerable, Courage, Determination
