Synopsis: In the heart of Pittsburgh's steel industry, the Scott family finds themselves caught in a web of love, loyalty, and class struggle. As Mary Rafferty, a young Irish maid, becomes entangled with the powerful Scott family, she must navigate the complexities of social hierarchy and forbidden romance. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, 'The Valley of Decision' explores the consequences of choices made in the face of societal expectations and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Love and sacrifice, Family dynamics
