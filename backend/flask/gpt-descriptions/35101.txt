Synopsis: 
'Diary of a U-Boat Commander' takes readers on a gripping journey through the eyes of a German submarine commander during World War I. Through the pages of his personal diary, we witness the intense battles, the camaraderie among the crew, and the moral dilemmas faced by the commander as he navigates the treacherous waters of war. As the diary unfolds, the reader becomes immersed in the harrowing experiences of life aboard a U-boat, where every decision could mean life or death. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Naval Fiction

Themes: War and its consequences, Morality and ethics in wartime, Camaraderie and loyalty, Survival and sacrifice
