Synopsis: 
Great Expectations is a captivating novel that follows the life of Pip, an orphaned boy who dreams of becoming a gentleman. Set in 19th-century England, the story takes us on a journey through Pip's encounters with eccentric characters, unexpected twists of fate, and the pursuit of love and identity. As Pip navigates the complexities of society, he discovers the true nature of his great expectations and the profound impact they have on his life.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Victorian literature

Themes: 
Social class, identity, ambition, love and loyalty
