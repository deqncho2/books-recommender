Synopsis: In 'Plague Ship' by Andre Norton, a deadly disease threatens to wipe out an entire planet, and a desperate race against time begins. When a group of interstellar traders becomes stranded on the infected planet, they must find a way to survive and prevent the disease from spreading to other worlds. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter mysterious beings, they uncover dark secrets that could change the fate of the entire galaxy. With danger lurking at every turn, the crew must rely on their resourcefulness and courage to overcome the plague and save themselves and countless others.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Sacrifice, Redemption
