Synopsis: 
'Twas the Night Before Christmas' tells the enchanting story of a magical visit from a jolly, bearded man on Christmas Eve. Set in a cozy home, the poem takes readers on a whimsical journey filled with anticipation, wonder, and the joy of the holiday season. As the night unfolds, the narrator's encounter with this mysterious figure brings a sense of awe and delight, leaving readers with a heartwarming message that captures the true spirit of Christmas.

Genres: 
Poetry, Children's Literature, Holiday

Themes: 
Christmas spirit, Joy and wonder, Generosity, Belief in magic
