Synopsis: 
'Alexander's Ragtime Band' is a captivating novel that takes readers on a journey through the vibrant world of music and love. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows the life of Alexander, a talented musician who forms a band that revolutionizes the music scene. As the band gains popularity, Alexander finds himself torn between his passion for music and the complexities of his personal relationships. With a backdrop of societal changes and the challenges of fame, the novel explores the power of music to bring people together and the sacrifices one must make to pursue their dreams.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Music

Themes: Love and relationships, Pursuit of dreams, Societal changes
