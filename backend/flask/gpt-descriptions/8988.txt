Synopsis: In 'The Master of the World' by Jules Verne, a series of mysterious events unfold as a strange and powerful machine terrorizes the world. As government officials and scientists race to uncover the truth behind this enigma, they find themselves facing a formidable adversary who seems to possess unimaginable powers. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, a thrilling adventure ensues, filled with suspense, danger, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Power and control, Exploration and discovery
