Synopsis: In Search of the Castaways follows the thrilling adventure of Lord Glenarvan and his crew as they embark on a perilous journey across the globe in search of a missing shipwrecked captain. With clues hidden in a bottle, they face treacherous landscapes, encounter fascinating cultures, and overcome numerous obstacles in their quest to rescue the castaways. As they delve deeper into their mission, they uncover secrets that will test their courage and determination. Will they succeed in their mission and bring the castaways back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Survival
