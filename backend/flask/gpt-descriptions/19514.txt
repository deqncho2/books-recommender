Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Princess' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, we follow the enchanting story of Sara Crewe, a young girl who finds herself in a world of privilege and luxury at Miss Minchin's boarding school. However, when a sudden turn of events leaves Sara penniless and at the mercy of her cruel headmistress, she must summon all her strength and resilience to overcome adversity and discover the true power of imagination and friendship.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Classic

Themes: Imagination, Resilience, Friendship, Kindness
