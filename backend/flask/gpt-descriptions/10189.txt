Synopsis: 
In 'Three Rubes Seeing New York' by Edison Vaudeville Company, three small-town friends embark on a thrilling adventure to the bustling city of New York. As they navigate the chaotic streets, encounter eccentric characters, and stumble upon unexpected situations, their naivety and innocence provide a refreshing perspective on the urban landscape. With their wide-eyed wonder and humorous mishaps, these three rubes bring laughter and heartwarming moments to the readers, making their journey through the city an unforgettable experience.

Genres: Comedy, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Innocence, Friendship, Culture Clash
