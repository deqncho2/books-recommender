Synopsis:
In "Ozma of Oz," Dorothy finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz, but this time she is accompanied by her beloved hen, Billina. As they explore this enchanting realm, they encounter new friends and face dangerous challenges, including the menacing Nome King who has captured the royal family of Ev. With the help of her companions, Dorothy must embark on a thrilling quest to rescue the missing royals and restore peace to the land of Oz.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Good vs. Evil
