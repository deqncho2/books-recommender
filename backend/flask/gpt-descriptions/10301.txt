Synopsis: In the small town of Ballymuck, a mysterious event unfolds when a pair of overalls is unexpectedly thrown into Mrs. Murphy's chowder during a community gathering. As the townspeople embark on a quest to uncover the culprit, secrets are revealed, suspicions arise, and unexpected alliances form. With humor, wit, and a touch of mischief, "Who Threw the Overalls in Mrs. Murphy's Chowder?" takes readers on a delightful journey through a charming Irish village, where everyone becomes a detective in this puzzling culinary caper.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Community, Secrets and deception
