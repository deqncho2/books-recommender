Synopsis: In the Bikini Island BAKER Atomic Test, a group of scientists and military personnel embark on a top-secret mission to a remote island. As they prepare to witness the detonation of a powerful atomic bomb, unexpected events unfold, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will challenge their beliefs and change their lives forever.

Genres: Thriller, Drama, Science Fiction

Themes: Nuclear warfare, Morality, Consequences of scientific advancements
