Synopsis: In 'The Iliad for Boys and Girls', Homer's epic tale of the Trojan War is retold in a captivating and accessible way, perfect for young readers. Follow the heroic adventures of Achilles, Hector, and the gods of Olympus as they clash in a battle that will shape the destiny of nations. Filled with thrilling battles, divine interventions, and complex characters, this timeless story of honor, glory, and the consequences of war will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Epic, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Honor, Glory, Fate, Loyalty, War
