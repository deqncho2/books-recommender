Synopsis: In the idyllic village of Little Hintock, a young woman named Grace Melbury finds herself torn between her childhood love, Giles Winterborne, and the allure of a sophisticated newcomer, Dr. Edred Fitzpiers. As their lives intertwine, secrets, desires, and societal expectations collide, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the fate of the Woodlanders.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, social class and expectations, the conflict between tradition and progress
