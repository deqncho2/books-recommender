Synopsis: In 'The Tiger' by William Blake, a mysterious and powerful creature lurks in the shadows, captivating the imagination of all who encounter it. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the tiger, they embark on a journey that explores the duality of nature and the profound questions it raises about the human condition. With vivid imagery and thought-provoking symbolism, this poetic masterpiece invites readers to contemplate the essence of existence and the complexities of the world we inhabit.

Genres: Poetry, Symbolism, Romanticism

Themes: Nature, Good vs. Evil, Human Nature, Creation, Religion
