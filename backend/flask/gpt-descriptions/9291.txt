Synopsis: 
In the enchanting world of Parisian high society, a young American widow finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and forbidden love. Anna Leath, seeking solace after her husband's untimely death, embarks on a journey that leads her to a luxurious villa on the French Riviera. There, she becomes captivated by the charismatic and enigmatic George Darrow, a man with a mysterious past and a reputation that precedes him. As their relationship deepens, Anna discovers that the idyllic paradise she has entered is teeming with hidden dangers and unexpected alliances. With her heart torn between loyalty and passion, Anna must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and self-discovery in order to find her true path.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, the pursuit of happiness, the clash between American and European cultures
