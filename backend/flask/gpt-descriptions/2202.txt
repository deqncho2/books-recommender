Synopsis: In 'Human Genome Project, rough draft, Chromosome Number 02', the Human Genome Project takes an unexpected turn as scientists uncover a startling revelation hidden within the second chromosome. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of our genetic code, they find themselves entangled in a web of ethical dilemmas and personal sacrifices. With each discovery, the boundaries of science and humanity blur, leaving the reader questioning the very essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science fiction, thriller, mystery

Themes: Genetic manipulation, ethics, identity
