Synopsis:
Moll Flanders is a captivating tale that follows the life of a resourceful and audacious woman who navigates through the turbulent world of 18th-century England. Born into poverty, Moll's journey takes her from the streets of London to the bustling city of Bath, and even across the Atlantic to the colonies of America. Along the way, she encounters a series of extraordinary circumstances, including multiple marriages, criminal exploits, and unexpected twists of fate. Through it all, Moll's indomitable spirit and cunning wit shine through, making her an unforgettable protagonist in this thrilling and morally complex narrative.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
Social mobility, Morality, Survival
