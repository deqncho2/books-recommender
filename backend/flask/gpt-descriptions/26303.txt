Synopsis:
In "Right Ho, Jeeves," the lovable and bumbling Bertie Wooster finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious misadventures. When his friend Gussie Fink-Nottle falls in love with a girl, Bertie is tasked with helping him win her heart. However, things quickly spiral out of control as misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and a stolen silver cow creamer come into play. With the ever-reliable Jeeves by his side, Bertie must navigate the chaos and find a way to set things right.

Genres:
Humor, Satire, Comedy

Themes:
Love and romance, Social class, Miscommunication
