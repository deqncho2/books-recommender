Synopsis: In "The Seven Poor Travellers," Charles Dickens takes us on a heartwarming journey during the holiday season. Set in a quaint English town, the story follows a group of destitute travelers who find themselves unexpectedly welcomed into a charitable institution. As they gather around a festive dinner table, each traveler shares their unique tale, revealing the hardships they have faced and the resilience that keeps them going. Through their stories, Dickens explores the power of compassion, the importance of community, and the transformative nature of kindness.

Genres: Fiction, Short Story, Christmas Literature

Themes: Compassion, Generosity, Community, Resilience
