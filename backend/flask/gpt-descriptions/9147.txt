Synopsis: In the heart of the 19th century, a young sailor named Ishmael embarks on a perilous journey aboard the whaling ship Pequod. Led by the enigmatic and vengeful Captain Ahab, the crew sets out to hunt down the legendary white whale known as Moby Dick. As the voyage unfolds, Ishmael becomes entangled in a world of obsession, madness, and the relentless pursuit of an elusive and formidable adversary.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Obsession, Revenge, Man vs. Nature, Madness, Existentialism
