Synopsis: In "The First Battle of Bull Run," General Beauregard takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the most significant battles of the American Civil War. Through vivid descriptions and personal accounts, the author brings to life the chaos, bravery, and strategic maneuvers that unfolded on the battlefield. As the Union and Confederate forces clash in a battle that will shape the course of the war, readers will be captivated by the intensity and unpredictability of the conflict.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Military

Themes: Courage, Strategy, Sacrifice, Brotherhood
