Synopsis: John Carter, a former Confederate soldier, mysteriously finds himself transported to the planet Mars, known as Barsoom to its inhabitants. There, he discovers a world filled with strange creatures, warring civilizations, and a beautiful princess in need of rescue. As Carter navigates this new and dangerous world, he must confront his own abilities and desires, ultimately becoming a hero in a battle for the fate of Barsoom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Heroism, Love and Sacrifice
