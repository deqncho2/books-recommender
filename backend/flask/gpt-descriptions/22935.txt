Synopsis: 
In 'The Second Epistle of John', the Apostle John pens a heartfelt letter to a chosen lady and her children, filled with wisdom and guidance. As he warns them about the dangers of false teachers and encourages them to remain steadfast in their faith, a sense of urgency and importance permeates his words. Through his eloquent prose, John imparts timeless truths that resonate with readers, urging them to hold fast to the love of God and walk in truth.

Genres: 
Religious, Epistolary

Themes: 
Faithfulness, Discernment, Love, Truth
