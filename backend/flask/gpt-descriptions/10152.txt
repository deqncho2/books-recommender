Synopsis: 
In 'Voice Trial - Kinetophone actor audition' by Lett, Bob, a struggling actor named Jack finds himself at a unique audition for a groundbreaking new technology called the Kinetophone. As he competes against other talented actors, Jack discovers that the audition process is far from ordinary, and the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping story takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of voice acting and the lengths one man will go to achieve his dreams.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Competition
