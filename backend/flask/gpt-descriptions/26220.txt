Synopsis: In the enchanting world of the Limberlost Swamp, a young girl named Elnora struggles to find her place amidst the shadows of loss and poverty. Determined to overcome her circumstances, she immerses herself in the wonders of nature, discovering a hidden world of beauty and secrets. As Elnora navigates the challenges of love, friendship, and self-discovery, she must confront the choices that will shape her future and uncover the truth about her own identity. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century Indiana, "A Girl of the Limberlost" is a captivating tale of resilience, redemption, and the transformative power of nature.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Nature, Identity, Perseverance, Social class
