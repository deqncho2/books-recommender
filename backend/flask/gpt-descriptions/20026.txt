Synopsis: In "Fables for the Frivolous," Guy Wetmore Carryl presents a collection of witty and whimsical fables that will transport readers to a world where animals and inanimate objects come to life. From a philosophical debate between a teapot and a kettle to a clever fox outsmarting a group of hunters, these fables are filled with humor, satire, and unexpected twists. Each story offers a delightful blend of entertainment and moral lessons, making it a captivating read for both children and adults.

Genres: Fiction, Fables, Humor

Themes: Morality, Satire, Wisdom
