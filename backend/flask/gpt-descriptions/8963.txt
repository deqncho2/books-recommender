Synopsis: In the gritty streets of New York City, Maggie, a young girl from a poverty-stricken family, struggles to find her place in a harsh and unforgiving world. As she navigates through the challenges of her environment, Maggie is faced with difficult choices that will shape her future. Stephen Crane's powerful and raw portrayal of Maggie's journey offers a poignant exploration of the human spirit and the consequences of societal constraints.

Genres: Realism, Naturalism, Social Commentary

Themes: Poverty, Morality, Social Determinism, Survival
