Synopsis: In this witty and satirical play, two young gentlemen, Jack Worthing and Algernon Moncrieff, create alter egos named Ernest to escape their mundane lives and win the hearts of their respective love interests. However, their web of lies becomes increasingly tangled when their deceptions are discovered, leading to hilarious misunderstandings and unexpected revelations. As the characters navigate the absurdities of Victorian society, Wilde's razor-sharp wit and clever wordplay take center stage, making this play a delightful and thought-provoking comedy of manners.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Identity and Deception, Social Class and Hypocrisy, Marriage and Love
