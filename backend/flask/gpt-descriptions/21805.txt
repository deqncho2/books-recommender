Synopsis: In the wake of a devastating attack on American soil, President Franklin D. Roosevelt delivers a powerful speech before the US Congress, revealing the shocking events that unfolded on December 7, 1941. With his words, he captures the attention of the nation, igniting a sense of unity and determination in the face of adversity. This historic speech marks a turning point in American history, forever altering the course of the nation's involvement in World War II.

Genres: Historical, Political, Speech

Themes: Patriotism, Leadership, Resilience, Unity
