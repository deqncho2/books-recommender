Synopsis: 
'From the Earth to the Moon' by Jules Verne takes readers on an exhilarating journey filled with scientific ingenuity, international competition, and the boundless human spirit. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a group of ambitious individuals who embark on a daring mission to send a projectile to the moon. As they face numerous challenges and encounter unexpected obstacles, the novel explores the power of human determination, the wonders of scientific exploration, and the unyielding pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Progress, Human Ambition
