Synopsis: In 'A Horse's Tale' by Mark Twain, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a horse named Soldier Boy. From his early days as a wild mustang to his adventures as a cavalry horse during the Indian Wars, Soldier Boy encounters a wide array of characters and experiences that shape his understanding of the world. With humor, wit, and a touch of satire, Twain weaves a tale that explores the complexities of human-animal relationships and challenges societal norms. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Satire

Themes: Animal intelligence, Friendship, Social commentary
