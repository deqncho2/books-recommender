Synopsis: 
Robinson Crusoe, a young and adventurous Englishman, sets sail on a voyage that takes an unexpected turn when his ship is wrecked in a storm. Stranded on a deserted island, Crusoe must learn to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment. With resourcefulness and determination, he builds a new life for himself, facing numerous challenges and encountering unexpected companions along the way. As the years pass, Crusoe's isolation forces him to confront his own beliefs, question his place in society, and ultimately discover the true meaning of resilience and self-reliance.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Fiction

Themes: Survival, Self-reliance, Isolation, Colonialism, Religion
