Synopsis: 
In 'The Glimpses of the Moon', we follow the enchanting story of Nick and Susy, two penniless but glamorous friends who devise a clever plan to live off the generosity of their wealthy acquaintances. As they embark on a whirlwind journey through the dazzling social scene of the early 20th century, they must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the true meaning of happiness. Will their scheme bring them the life they desire, or will they discover that true wealth lies in unexpected places?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Social Satire

Themes: Love and relationships, Wealth and social status, Self-discovery
