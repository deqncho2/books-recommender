Synopsis: In 'The Red House Mystery', a wealthy man invites his old friend to his country estate for a weekend getaway. However, when a mysterious murder occurs, the two friends find themselves caught up in a thrilling investigation to uncover the truth. With a cast of intriguing characters and a web of secrets, this classic whodunit will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Trust and betrayal, Deception, Justice and truth
