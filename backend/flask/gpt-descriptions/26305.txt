Synopsis: In the heartwarming tale of 'Yes, Virginia, There Is A Santa Claus', a young girl named Virginia questions the existence of Santa Claus, prompting her to seek answers from the most unexpected source. As the story unfolds, Virginia embarks on a journey of discovery, hope, and belief that will touch the hearts of readers young and old.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Holiday

Themes: Belief, Imagination, Hope, the Power of Faith
