Synopsis: 
In "King Grisly-Beard," a young princess is forced to marry a hideous and mysterious king, known as King Grisly-Beard. Determined to prove her worth, she embarks on a series of challenges and tasks set by the king, facing danger and uncovering secrets along the way. As the princess's journey unfolds, she discovers the true nature of King Grisly-Beard and learns valuable lessons about love, appearances, and the power of inner beauty.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Love and marriage, Inner beauty, Appearances can be deceiving
