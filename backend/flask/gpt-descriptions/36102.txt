Synopsis: In 'A Princess of Mars', John Carter, a Confederate veteran, mysteriously finds himself transported to the planet Mars. There, he discovers a world filled with strange creatures, warring civilizations, and a captivating princess in need of rescue. As Carter navigates this new and dangerous world, he must confront his own abilities and desires, ultimately finding himself torn between loyalty and love.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
