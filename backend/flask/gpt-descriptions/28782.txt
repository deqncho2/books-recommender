Synopsis:
In 'Legend Land, Volume 1 & 2', G. Basil Barham takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of folklore and mythology. From the enchanting tales of King Arthur and his knights to the haunting legends of ghosts and spirits, this collection of stories is sure to ignite the imagination. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a world filled with adventure, magic, and mystery. As the characters navigate through ancient forests, encounter mythical creatures, and face formidable challenges, they will discover the power of courage, honor, and the enduring nature of legends.

Genres:
Fantasy, Folklore, Mythology

Themes:
Adventure, Magic, Courage, Honor, Legends
