Synopsis: In 'Through Space to Mars; Or, the Longest Journey on Record' by Rockwood, Roy, a group of adventurous scientists embark on an extraordinary mission to Mars. As they navigate the vastness of space, they encounter unforeseen challenges and thrilling discoveries that will test their courage and determination. With each passing moment, the stakes grow higher, and the fate of their mission hangs in the balance. Will they succeed in reaching the red planet, or will their journey end in disaster?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Scientific Discovery
