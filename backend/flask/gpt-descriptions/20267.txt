Synopsis: In "At The Bay" by Katherine Mansfield, readers are transported to a picturesque bay where the Burnell family spends their summer holidays. Through the eyes of various family members and their acquaintances, we witness the complexities of their relationships, the secrets they hold, and the subtle power dynamics that shape their interactions. As the day unfolds, the idyllic setting becomes a backdrop for hidden desires, unspoken tensions, and unexpected revelations. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Story

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and hidden desires, Power dynamics
