Synopsis: In 'Human Genome Project, rough draft, Chromosome Number 06', the Human Genome Project takes an unexpected turn as scientists uncover a hidden code within the sixth chromosome. As they delve deeper into this mysterious genetic sequence, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, ethical dilemmas, and the potential for groundbreaking discoveries. With each revelation, the boundaries of science and humanity blur, leaving the characters questioning the very essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Genetic manipulation, Ethics, Identity
