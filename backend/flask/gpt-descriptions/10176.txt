Synopsis: In 'The Debutante: Caprice Brillante', a young woman named Amelia finds herself thrust into the glamorous world of high society as she prepares for her debutante ball. As she navigates the intricate web of etiquette, secrets, and rivalries, Amelia discovers that there is much more at stake than just her social standing. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the lengths one will go to protect their reputation and the true cost of ambition.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Social hierarchy, Identity, Betrayal
