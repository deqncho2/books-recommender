Synopsis: In 'The House of Mapuhi' by Jack London, a young pearl diver named Mapuhi dreams of finding a rare and valuable pearl that will change his life forever. When he finally discovers the pearl of his dreams, he becomes consumed by greed and must navigate the treacherous waters of the pearl trade. As Mapuhi's obsession grows, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected consequences that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Greed, Ambition, Betrayal, Consequences
