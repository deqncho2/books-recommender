Synopsis: 
'The Odyssey' by Homer is an epic tale that follows the adventures of the hero Odysseus as he attempts to return home after the Trojan War. Facing numerous obstacles and encountering mythical creatures, Odysseus must navigate through treacherous waters and overcome his own flaws in order to reunite with his family. This timeless story of perseverance and the power of the human spirit will keep readers captivated from beginning to end.

Genres: Epic, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Heroism, Homecoming, Fate, Loyalty, Cunning
