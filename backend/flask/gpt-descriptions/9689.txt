Synopsis: In "Three Ghost Stories," Charles Dickens takes readers on a haunting journey through three chilling tales. From a mysterious specter that haunts a railway signalman to a ghostly encounter in an abandoned house, and a haunting visitation on Christmas Eve, these stories will leave readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries between the living and the dead.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, horror

Themes: The supernatural, the power of guilt, the consequences of past actions, the fragility of the human mind
