Synopsis: In the midst of Victorian society, a young man named Daniel Deronda finds himself torn between two worlds: the aristocratic circles he was raised in and the mysterious Jewish community he becomes drawn to. As he navigates his own identity and purpose, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and self-discovery that will challenge everything he thought he knew about himself and the world around him.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Love and relationships, Social class, Religion and spirituality
