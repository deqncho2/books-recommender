Synopsis: In 'Current Superstitions', Fanny D. Bergen delves into the fascinating world of superstitions, exploring the beliefs and practices that have captivated human minds for centuries. From ancient rituals to modern-day customs, this book takes readers on a journey through the intriguing and often mysterious realm of superstition. With a blend of historical anecdotes, personal experiences, and cultural analysis, Bergen offers a thought-provoking exploration of why superstitions persist in our rational world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cultural Studies, Folklore

Themes: Superstition, Belief Systems, Cultural Traditions
