Synopsis: 
In "My Mark Twain," William Dean Howells takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal friendship with the legendary American author, Mark Twain. Through a series of vivid anecdotes and intimate conversations, Howells offers a unique and insightful perspective on Twain's life, humor, and literary genius. As the two writers navigate the complexities of fame, success, and the ever-changing landscape of American literature, readers are treated to a delightful and thought-provoking exploration of friendship, creativity, and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Criticism

Themes: Friendship, Creativity, Fame, American Literature
