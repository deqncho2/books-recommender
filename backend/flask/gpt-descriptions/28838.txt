Synopsis: 
The Getting of Wisdom by Henry Handel Richardson is a captivating coming-of-age novel set in late 19th century Australia. The story follows the journey of Laura Rambotham, a young girl from a rural background, as she navigates her way through a prestigious boarding school in Melbourne. As Laura encounters the challenges of fitting in, making friends, and striving for academic success, she also grapples with the complexities of social hierarchies, societal expectations, and the pursuit of personal identity. With wit, humor, and a keen eye for human nature, Richardson paints a vivid portrait of adolescence and the universal quest for wisdom.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Social hierarchy, Education, Self-discovery
