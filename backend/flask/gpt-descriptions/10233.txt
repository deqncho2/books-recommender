Synopsis: In 'Transcontinental Telephone Address to Thomas A. Edison', Hutchinson takes readers on a captivating journey through time and space as he explores the possibilities of communication. Through a transcontinental telephone call, the protagonist engages in a thought-provoking conversation with the renowned inventor, Thomas A. Edison. As they delve into the realms of science, technology, and human connection, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eager to discover the outcome.

Genres: Science fiction, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Communication, Innovation, Human connection
