Synopsis: In "I Heard the Bells on Christmas Day" by McGuinn, Roger, a young musician finds himself caught in the chaos of the holiday season. As he navigates through personal struggles and societal unrest, he discovers the power of music to heal and bring people together. With each note he plays, he uncovers a deeper understanding of the true meaning of Christmas.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Music, Holiday

Themes: Redemption, Hope, Unity, the Power of Music
