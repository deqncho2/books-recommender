Synopsis: In 'Human Genome Project, X Chromosome', the groundbreaking research of the Human Genome Project takes a fascinating turn as scientists delve into the mysteries of the X chromosome. As they unravel the secrets hidden within this crucial piece of genetic code, they uncover astonishing revelations that challenge our understanding of human biology and evolution. With each discovery, the stakes rise higher, leading to a gripping exploration of the profound impact the X chromosome has on our lives.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Genetics

Themes: Human biology, Genetics, Evolution
