Synopsis:
'Little Wars' is a captivating and imaginative work by H. G. Wells that takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of miniature warfare. In this delightful book, Wells introduces a group of friends who gather together to engage in a unique form of play: waging war with toy soldiers. What starts as innocent fun soon evolves into a gripping and strategic battle, filled with unexpected twists and turns. As the friends immerse themselves in their miniature world, they discover the true power of imagination and the profound impact it can have on their lives. 

Genres:
Adventure, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Imagination, Friendship, Escapism
