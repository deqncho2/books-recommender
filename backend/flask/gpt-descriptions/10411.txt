Synopsis: 
'By the Sapphire Sea' is a captivating tale set in a small coastal town, where secrets lie beneath the surface of idyllic beauty. When a mysterious stranger arrives, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As the story unfolds, a web of intrigue, love, and betrayal is woven, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With its richly developed characters and atmospheric setting, this novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
