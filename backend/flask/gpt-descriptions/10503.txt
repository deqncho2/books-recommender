Synopsis: In 'John Henry' by McGuinn, Roger, a young man named John Henry embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the heart of the American West. Along the way, he encounters a cast of colorful characters, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers a hidden world filled with mystery and adventure. As John Henry delves deeper into this captivating world, he must confront his own fears and uncover the truth about his own identity. This thrilling tale of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's destiny will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Courage
