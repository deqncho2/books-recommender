Synopsis: 
Phileas Fogg, a wealthy and eccentric Englishman, makes a daring wager with his fellow club members that he can travel around the world in just eighty days. With his loyal French valet, Passepartout, by his side, Fogg embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected obstacles, breathtaking landscapes, and encounters with fascinating characters from different cultures. As the clock ticks and the challenges mount, Fogg's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he succeed in his audacious quest and prove that the world can be conquered in eighty days?

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Differences
